Frequently Asked Questions About Street Team Marketing

How much does a street team cost?

Street team costs depend on the city, campaign type, team size, and staff experience level. We provide custom, all-inclusive quotes with no hidden fees. Request a free quote.

What cities do your street teams cover?

We operate in over 1,000 cities across all 50 US states. Our strongest presence is in NYC, LA, Chicago, Miami, Dallas, Atlanta, Houston, Denver, Phoenix, San Francisco, Seattle, Boston, Nashville, and Las Vegas. 48-hour rush deployment available. View all locations.

How do I hire a street team?

Contact us with your campaign details. We provide a custom quote within 24 hours, recruit and train your team, then deploy with GPS tracking and real-time reporting. Most campaigns are live within 5-7 business days. Learn how it works.

What is the difference between a street team and brand ambassadors?

Street teams focus on high-volume tactics like flyer distribution and canvassing. Brand ambassadors engage consumers with deeper product knowledge at events and retail locations. Read the full comparison.

Do you provide event staffing for trade shows?

Yes. We staff trade shows, festivals, corporate events, and sporting events nationwide. Our staff handle registration, demos, crowd management, and VIP hospitality for events of 50 to 50,000+ attendees. Learn about event staffing.
