## After Listing: Get Reviews

The single biggest boost on Clutch and GoodFirms is verified client reviews. After creating profiles:

1. Email 5-10 past clients asking for a review
2. Provide direct links to your Clutch/GoodFirms/Google profiles
3. Offer to make it easy (Clutch does phone interviews, ~15 min)
4. Even 3-5 reviews puts you ahead of most competitors on these platforms
