Our Story: Why Street Teams Co Exists

Street Teams Co was founded to solve a problem that every marketing manager and brand director has experienced firsthand: unreliable field marketing. The story goes something like this. You hire a staffing agency. They promise a team of ten. Seven show up. Three of those seven are unprepared. One leaves early. The report you receive three days later is a spreadsheet with vague numbers and no photos. You have no idea whether the campaign actually worked, and you have spent weeks of your time managing an agency that was supposed to manage things for you.

We lived this story ourselves while working on the brand side. Campaign after campaign, market after market, the same problems kept appearing: no-shows, undertrained staff, zero real-time visibility, and reporting that required a leap of faith to believe. The field marketing industry was stuck in the past while digital marketing had evolved into a precision instrument with real-time dashboards, granular attribution, and optimization on the fly.

So we asked a question that seemed obvious but nobody was answering: why can't in-person marketing work like digital? Why can't you see your team on a map in real time? Why can't you get photo verification and engagement data as it happens? Why can't you calculate cost-per-interaction the same way you calculate cost-per-click? Why can't the agency guarantee that the people who are supposed to show up actually show up?

Street Teams Co is the answer to those questions. We built a street team marketing agency from the ground up around three principles that the traditional staffing industry had ignored for decades: technology-driven accountability, rigorous staff vetting, and marketing-first thinking. We are not a temp agency that happens to do events. We are a marketing company that happens to staff people.

"Our mission is to make in-person marketing as measurable and scalable as digital marketing. Every interaction tracked. Every dollar justified. Every campaign optimized."

Our Approach: Technology-Driven Field Marketing

What separates Street Teams Co from traditional staffing agencies is not just better people, it is better systems. We built our technology platform from the ground up to give brands the same level of visibility and control over field marketing that they expect from their digital campaigns. Here is how our technology-driven approach works at every stage of a campaign.

GPS Tracking & Verification

Every team member checks in and out via GPS-enabled mobile app. You see real-time locations on a live map, verify arrival times, and monitor activity throughout each shift. No more trusting that people showed up because someone told you they did.

Real-Time Client Dashboard

Your client portal shows live campaign data: staff locations, check-in times, photo uploads, engagement counts, and KPI progress. Access from any device, any time. Daily automated reports hit your inbox at end of shift.

Show-Up Guarantee

Our triple-confirmation system contacts every team member at 72 hours, 24 hours, and 2 hours before each shift. If someone cannot make it, we activate a standby replacement immediately. You never pay for unfilled positions.

Performance Analytics

Post-campaign analysis with cost-per-interaction, cost-per-lead, market comparisons, staff performance rankings, and strategic recommendations. We turn field data into actionable intelligence for future campaigns.

Staff Performance Ratings

Every staff member receives performance ratings after every campaign. Top performers are prioritized for future work. Underperformers are coached or removed. This creates a self-improving talent network that gets better with every campaign.

Scalable Operations

Our platform manages staffing, scheduling, training, logistics, and reporting across hundreds of simultaneous campaigns in 1,000+ cities. Scale from five people in one city to fifty across ten markets with one point of contact.

Our Values

The way we run Street Teams Co reflects a set of values that guide every decision, from how we vet staff to how we communicate with clients:

  • Accountability over promises: We do not ask you to trust us. We give you GPS data, photo verification, and real-time dashboards so you can see for yourself. If something goes wrong, we own it immediately and fix it before you have to ask.
  • Results over activity: Distributing 10,000 flyers means nothing if nobody reads them. We measure what matters to your business: conversions, leads, trial rates, and ROI. Activity metrics are a means to an end, not the end itself.
  • Transparency over optics: Our pricing is honest and itemized — every quote breaks out exactly what you are paying for. Our reporting is real-time. Our staff profiles are honest. We would rather give you accurate information and a realistic plan than oversell and underdeliver.
  • Continuous improvement over status quo: Every campaign generates data that makes the next campaign better. We analyze what worked, what did not, and why. Clients who run recurring programs see measurable improvement in performance metrics over time.
  • Partnership over transaction: We succeed when our clients succeed. That means proactively recommending changes, flagging potential issues, and investing in your long-term marketing success rather than just fulfilling today's order.
