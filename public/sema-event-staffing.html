Frequently Asked Questions

How much does SEMA booth staffing cost?

SEMA staffing is custom-quoted based on roles, headcount, show days, and hours. Product specialists and promotional talent price differently, and SEMA week is a premium Vegas market. Tell us your booth size and goals and we will send a free itemized quote.

When should I book staff for SEMA 2026?

As early as possible. SEMA runs the first week of November and shares the Las Vegas talent pool with a packed fall trade-show calendar. Booking by late summer gets you experienced SEMA veterans; late requests mean limited selection.

Do your staff know the automotive industry?

We match staff to your product category — wheels and tires, performance, restyling, tools, or collision — and every staffer completes your product training before day one. For deeply technical demos we pair your engineers with hosts who manage flow and capture leads.

Can you staff SEMA Ignited and off-site events?

Yes. We staff SEMA Ignited, hotel and casino activations, cruise-in events, and Strip street teams during show week, with Las Vegas permitting handled.

Do you provide bilingual staff for SEMA?

Yes. SEMA draws international distributors and buyers, and we staff ambassadors fluent in Spanish, Mandarin, Japanese, German, and other languages on request.
