Introduction

Elev8 Staffing (also branded Eleven8) and Street Teams Co both place professional promotional talent for brands, but they serve different needs. Elev8 is a Los Angeles-based experiential and event staffing agency known for premium, hand-curated talent and strong work in the LA market — festivals like Coachella, hospitality, trade shows, and brand activations. Street Teams Co is a nationwide street team marketing agency operating in 1,000+ US cities across all 50 states, with eight service categories that extend well beyond event staffing into guerrilla marketing, product sampling, flyer distribution, and experiential activations. This comparison is written to help brand and marketing managers understand where each agency excels. We aim to be fair: Elev8 brings genuine strengths in curated LA-market staffing, and Street Teams Co brings nationwide scale and full-funnel field marketing.

Coverage and Reach

The clearest difference is geographic. Elev8 concentrates on Los Angeles and Southern California, with deep familiarity in the LA event, festival, and hospitality scene (including festival markets like Indio/Coachella). For a brand running a premium activation in Los Angeles, that local depth is an asset. Street Teams Co maintains active, vetted staff networks in 1,000+ cities across all 50 states. Brands can launch simultaneous campaigns in Los Angeles, Miami, Chicago, and Denver with local teams in each market, all managed through one account. For LA-only events, Elev8 is a capable local specialist; for multi-city tours, national product launches, or simultaneous deployment across many markets, Street Teams Co's nationwide infrastructure is the practical choice. Notably, Street Teams Co also provides Los Angeles 2028 activation coverage for brands planning around the 2028 Summer Games.

Services Offered

Elev8 focuses on experiential and event staffing — brand ambassadors, promotional models, greeters, hospitality staff, and activation support, with particular strength in festivals and premium LA events. Street Teams Co offers eight distinct service categories spanning the full grassroots spectrum: street team activations, brand ambassadors, event staffing, product sampling, guerrilla marketing, flyer distribution, experiential marketing, and promotional staffing. If your campaign is purely premium event staffing in LA, Elev8 covers it well. If you need product sampling tours, guerrilla activations, or street teams in addition to event staff — especially across markets — Street Teams Co handles the full program from one agency.

Talent and Quality

Elev8 earns real credit here: the agency is known for hand-curating premium talent and matching ambassadors carefully to each brand and event, which is well-suited to high-profile LA activations where presentation and polish matter most. Street Teams Co applies a structured vetting and training process across all eight service categories nationwide — background checks, communication assessments, campaign-specific training, and a performance rating system that tracks reliability and client feedback across assignments. The Show-Up Guarantee adds contractual accountability: if a confirmed staffer does not arrive, Street Teams Co provides a replacement or credits the client. For boutique, premium LA events, Elev8's curation model is a strength; for consistent quality at scale across many markets, Street Teams Co's systematized process and guarantee provide reliability.

Technology and Reporting

Street Teams Co provides every client a real-time analytics dashboard with live GPS tracking of field staff, timestamped and geotagged photos, engagement counts, and distribution metrics — visibility that is especially valuable when running campaigns across several cities simultaneously. This real-time layer lets brands verify field operations as they happen and make mid-campaign adjustments. For brands managing large budgets or reporting to executives across multiple markets, this transparency is a meaningful advantage over standard post-event reporting.
