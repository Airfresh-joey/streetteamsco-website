Mosaic Experiential vs Street Teams Co: Full Comparison 2026

A thorough comparison of a large-scale experiential marketing firm and an agile, nationwide street team marketing agency. We compare scale, services, technology, pricing, and flexibility to help you select the right partner.

Overview: Mosaic Experiential and Street Teams Co

Mosaic Experiential and Street Teams Co operate in overlapping but distinct segments of the field marketing industry. Mosaic Experiential, part of the Acosta Group family of companies, is one of the largest experiential and shopper marketing agencies in North America. They operate at enterprise scale, providing retail merchandising, in-store product demonstrations, shopper marketing, experiential activations, and consumer engagement programs for major CPG and retail brands. With thousands of field representatives and deep relationships with national retail chains, Mosaic is built for Fortune 500 companies running massive, ongoing consumer engagement programs.

Street Teams Co is an independent street team marketing agency that provides eight categories of grassroots field marketing services across 1,000+ US cities. While smaller in overall corporate size, Street Teams Co offers advantages in agility, pricing transparency, service breadth beyond retail environments, and personalized client service. Their real-time GPS tracking platform, Show-Up Guarantee, and custom-quoted proposals represent a modern approach to field marketing that prioritizes accountability and client control.

This comparison will help marketing managers and brand teams understand the trade-offs between a large corporate field marketing operation and an agile, technology-forward independent agency. The right choice depends on campaign scope, budget structure, service requirements, and how much hands-on attention you need from your agency partner.

Detailed Analysis: Services and Specialization

Mosaic Experiential Services

Mosaic Experiential's service portfolio is built around the retail and shopper marketing ecosystem. Their core offerings include in-store product demonstrations, retail merchandising and reset teams, shopper marketing campaigns, end-cap and display installations, and experiential brand activations at retail locations and events. They excel in programs where brands need tens of thousands of product demonstrations executed across Walmart, Target, Costco, and other national retail chains simultaneously. Their infrastructure for managing retail relationships, compliance, and high-volume staffing is difficult to match. Mosaic also offers experiential marketing services for brand activations and consumer engagement outside of retail, though this is a secondary offering compared to their retail core.

Street Teams Co Services

Street Teams Co's eight service categories span a wider range of field marketing environments: street team activations, brand ambassadors, event staffing, product sampling, guerrilla marketing, flyer distribution, experiential marketing, and promotional staffing. While Street Teams Co handles product sampling at retail locations, they also deploy in environments that Mosaic does not typically cover: street-level guerrilla activations, festival and event grounds, college campuses, downtown business districts, nightlife venues, and outdoor public spaces. This versatility makes Street Teams Co the more flexible option for campaigns that need to reach consumers beyond the retail shelf.

Detailed Analysis: Scale vs. Agility

The core trade-off between these agencies is scale versus agility. Mosaic's corporate infrastructure, backed by Acosta Group's resources, enables them to manage programs involving thousands of field representatives executing simultaneously across national retail chains. If your campaign requires 5,000 in-store demonstrations on a single weekend across Costco locations nationwide, Mosaic has the infrastructure for that level of execution. However, this scale comes with corporate overhead, longer procurement processes, higher minimum budgets, and less flexibility for custom or unconventional campaign formats.

Street Teams Co operates with the agility of an independent agency while maintaining the geographic reach of a national player. Campaigns can be launched within 48 hours in major markets. There are no long-term contract requirements. Proposals are delivered quickly, and a dedicated account manager handles your campaign personally rather than routing requests through corporate layers. For brands that need to move fast, test new markets, run unconventional campaigns, or simply want a more hands-on relationship with their agency, Street Teams Co's structure delivers responsiveness that large corporate agencies cannot match.

Detailed Analysis: Pricing and Accessibility

Mosaic Experiential works primarily with enterprise-level brands and typically requires significant minimum campaign commitments. Their pricing reflects the infrastructure behind their operations including field management, retail relationships, compliance systems, and corporate overhead. For brands with seven-figure annual field marketing budgets, Mosaic's pricing aligns with their scale. For mid-market brands, emerging consumer companies, or agencies managing campaigns with more modest budgets, the entry point can be prohibitive.

Street Teams Co provides custom-quoted rates tailored to each role and market. There are no minimum campaign commitments, no long-term contracts required, and proposals are fully itemized so brands understand exactly where every dollar goes. This accessibility makes professional field marketing available to a wider range of brands, from startups launching their first sampling campaign to Fortune 500 companies running targeted activations where corporate-scale infrastructure is unnecessary. The custom-quote model also simplifies vendor comparison and internal budget approval processes.

Detailed Analysis: Technology

Both agencies invest heavily in technology, but their platforms serve different use cases. Mosaic's technology stack is built for retail environments, including planogram compliance verification, retail display auditing, shopper analytics, and point-of-sale data integration. These tools are essential for brands measuring the impact of in-store merchandising and demonstration programs on retail sell-through metrics.

Street Teams Co's technology platform is built for field marketing operations across diverse environments. Real-time GPS tracking shows the exact location of every field team member during active campaigns. Timestamped, geotagged photo uploads verify on-site presence and activity. Live consumer engagement counts and sample distribution metrics update throughout the day. A client-accessible dashboard consolidates all data in one view. This platform is designed to answer the fundamental question that haunts field marketing: "Are my people actually there, doing what they are supposed to be doing?" For brands running street teams, brand ambassador deployments, guerrilla activations, or event staffing, this real-time visibility is significantly more relevant than retail planogram analytics.

Who Should Choose Mosaic Experiential

Mosaic Experiential is the right choice for large CPG brands that need massive-scale retail execution. If your primary need is in-store product demonstrations across national retail chains, retail merchandising and reset programs, or shopper marketing campaigns integrated with retailer data, Mosaic's infrastructure is built specifically for that purpose. Their relationships with major retailers, their compliance systems, and their ability to deploy thousands of field representatives simultaneously make them an industry leader in retail-focused field marketing. Brands with annual field marketing budgets in the seven figures and programs requiring ongoing, year-round retail coverage will benefit most from Mosaic's capabilities.

Who Should Choose Street Teams Co

Street Teams Co is the better choice for brands that need versatile field marketing beyond the retail environment, value pricing transparency and flexibility, or want the responsiveness of an independent agency with national reach. Whether your campaign involves brand ambassadors at trade shows, street teams at festivals, product sampling at farmers markets and community events, guerrilla marketing in urban cores, or event staffing at corporate functions, Street Teams Co handles it all with custom-quoted rates and no minimum commitment. The Show-Up Guarantee eliminates staffing reliability risk, and the real-time GPS dashboard provides accountability that even Mosaic's enterprise clients rarely experience. For mid-market brands, emerging companies, or any brand that wants to move fast without corporate procurement delays, Street Teams Co delivers professional field marketing with modern transparency.
