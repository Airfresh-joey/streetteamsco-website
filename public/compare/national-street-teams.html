About National Street Teams

National Street Teams is a street team marketing company that provides brand ambassadors and promotional staff for grassroots marketing campaigns. They focus on street-level promotions including flyer distribution, product sampling, and face-to-face consumer engagement in major US markets.

National Street Teams has experience deploying field marketing teams for consumer brands, entertainment companies, and local businesses. Their core offering centers on putting promotional staff on the street to create direct consumer interactions.

About Street Teams Co

Street Teams Co is a full-service street team marketing agency offering eight distinct service categories in 1,000+ cities. Beyond traditional street team deployments, Street Teams Co provides brand ambassador programs, event staffing, product sampling, guerrilla marketing, flyer distribution, experiential marketing, and promotional staffing.

Key Differences

Service Breadth

While both agencies provide street team services, Street Teams Co offers a significantly broader range of marketing services. For brands running campaigns that combine street teams with event staffing, product sampling, or guerrilla activations, Street Teams Co provides a single-agency solution that eliminates the need for multiple vendor relationships.

Technology and Reporting

Street Teams Co provides real-time GPS tracking, timestamped photo uploads, live engagement data, and analytics dashboards accessible to clients during active campaigns. This technology layer gives brands visibility into field operations as they happen, enabling real-time adjustments and accountability.

Pricing Transparency

Street Teams Co publishes rate ranges starting at $25/hr and provides itemized proposals that break down staff costs, management fees, and additional charges. This transparency simplifies budgeting and agency comparison for brands evaluating multiple partners.

Scale and Speed

With 10,000+ vetted professionals across 1,000+ cities, Street Teams Co can deploy teams rapidly, including 48-hour rush staffing in major metros. This scale enables simultaneous multi-city campaigns and last-minute staffing needs that smaller networks may struggle to accommodate.

Who Should Choose Which?

Choose Street Teams Co if: You need comprehensive grassroots marketing services, nationwide multi-city coverage, transparent pricing, real-time campaign tracking, and fast deployment. Best for brands running multi-service, multi-market campaigns.
