Interactions Marketing vs Street Teams Co | 2026 Comparison

Interactions Marketing (formerly Daymon Interactions) dominates in-store product demos at warehouse clubs. Street Teams Co provides full-service grassroots marketing across 1,000+ cities. This honest comparison covers pricing, services, coverage, and technology to help you choose the right agency.

Introduction: In-Store Demos vs. Full-Service Grassroots Marketing

Interactions Marketing, formerly known as Daymon Interactions and a subsidiary of Daymon Worldwide (now part of Advantage Solutions), is the largest in-store product demonstration company in the United States. They are the brand behind the sample stations at Costco, Sam's Club, and BJ's Wholesale Club that millions of shoppers encounter every weekend. Their expertise is singular and deep: getting products sampled and sold inside wholesale club environments. Street Teams Co operates across a fundamentally different canvas. As a nationwide grassroots marketing agency with eight service categories and staff in 1,000+ cities, Street Teams Co handles everything from outdoor product sampling at farmer's markets and festivals to street team deployments around concert venues to event staffing at trade shows. These two agencies serve different needs and different moments in the consumer journey. This comparison will help you understand which one, or whether both, belongs in your marketing mix.

Services and Scope

Interactions Marketing does one thing extremely well: in-store product demonstrations. They operate demo stations inside Costco, Sam's Club, and BJ's Wholesale Club locations across the country, managing the entire process from staffing and food safety compliance to sample preparation and consumer engagement. Their scale is impressive, with thousands of demo associates conducting millions of product samples annually. For CPG brands seeking trial and purchase at the point of sale inside warehouse clubs, Interactions is the category leader.

Street Teams Co's scope is deliberately broader. With eight service categories, including product sampling that happens outside retail environments, Street Teams Co reaches consumers where they live, work, and play. Outdoor sampling at farmer's markets, festivals, gyms, college campuses, and busy pedestrian areas. Guerrilla marketing stunts that generate social media buzz. Brand ambassador deployments at events and experiential activations. Event staffing at trade shows and corporate functions. This breadth means brands can build comprehensive grassroots campaigns through a single agency rather than cobbling together multiple vendors for different tactics and environments.

Pricing Comparison

Interactions Marketing operates on a program-based pricing model tied to their warehouse club partnerships. Costs are typically structured per demo event or per program, negotiated between the CPG brand and Interactions (sometimes with the retailer involved). This enterprise model is designed for brands with national retail distribution and significant sampling budgets. Pricing is not publicly available, and program minimums reflect the scale of national warehouse club rollouts.

Street Teams Co provides custom-quoted hourly rates tailored to each role and market. Campaigns are custom-quoted by role and market, making professional grassroots marketing accessible to brands at any stage. Every proposal is fully itemized, with no hidden charges. For brands evaluating the cost-effectiveness of in-store demos versus outdoor sampling or street team activations, Street Teams Co's transparent pricing makes direct ROI comparisons straightforward.

Technology and Accountability

Interactions Marketing tracks sampling activity at the program level, including samples distributed, demo events completed, and program compliance metrics. Their reporting serves the needs of CPG brand managers who need to justify sampling program ROI to retail buyers and internal stakeholders. The data is retail-focused: how many consumers sampled the product, and what happened to sales velocity during and after the demo period.

Street Teams Co's technology platform provides a different layer of visibility. Every field campaign includes real-time GPS tracking of all staff positions, timestamped and geotagged photo uploads, live consumer engagement counts, distribution metrics, and lead capture data, all accessible through a client analytics dashboard. This level of transparency matters for outdoor activations where you cannot physically observe your team's performance the way a store manager can observe in-store demo staff. For marketing directors managing budgets across multiple agencies and channels, Street Teams Co's real-time data provides immediate accountability and mid-campaign optimization opportunities.

The Case for Using Both Agencies

Smart CPG brands do not view in-store demos and outdoor grassroots marketing as competing channels. They view them as complementary touchpoints in a consumer's path to purchase. Interactions Marketing drives trial and impulse purchase at the point of sale. Street Teams Co builds awareness, generates trial, and creates brand affinity in the moments before a consumer enters the store. A new kombucha brand might deploy Street Teams Co to sample at yoga studios, farmer's markets, and outdoor fitness events where their target consumers gather, building awareness and preference. Simultaneously, they run Interactions demo programs at Costco to convert that awareness into bulk purchase. The outdoor sampling creates demand; the in-store demo captures it. Using both agencies for different stages of the consumer journey is not redundant, it is a proven integrated strategy that maximizes both trial and conversion.

When to Choose Interactions Marketing

Choose Interactions Marketing when your primary goal is in-store product trial and purchase at Costco, Sam's Club, or BJ's Wholesale Club. Their exclusive demo partnerships with these retailers, combined with their scale and food safety compliance infrastructure, make them the clear leader for warehouse club sampling. If you have a CPG product with national wholesale club distribution and a budget to support a multi-store demo program, Interactions delivers the most efficient path to in-store trial and velocity lifts.

When to Choose Street Teams Co

Choose Street Teams Co when your sampling and marketing strategy extends beyond the warehouse club aisle. Street Teams Co is the right agency when:

  • You need outdoor or event-based sampling: Farmer's markets, festivals, campuses, gyms, parks, and street-level venues
  • Your campaign includes non-sampling tactics: Street teams, guerrilla marketing, brand ambassadors, event staffing, and experiential activations
  • You need multi-city coordination: Single account manager across 1,000+ cities versus managing demo schedules at individual club locations
  • Your budget needs flexibility: Custom-quoted campaigns with itemized, transparent pricing
  • Real-time field visibility matters: GPS tracking and live dashboards for every outdoor activation
  • You want reliability guarantees: Show-Up Guarantee ensures your team is in place when and where promised
  • You need fast turnaround: 48-hour rush staffing for time-sensitive opportunities

For brands building awareness outside retail or integrating street-level marketing with their existing demo programs, Street Teams Co's product sampling services provide the reach, versatility, and accountability that in-store-only agencies cannot match.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does Interactions Marketing compare to Street Teams Co?

Interactions Marketing is the largest in-store product demonstration company in the US, operating primarily inside Costco, Sam's Club, and BJ's. Street Teams Co is a full-service street team and grassroots marketing agency providing eight service categories across 1,000+ US cities. Interactions is the specialist for warehouse club demos; Street Teams Co is the versatile choice for multi-channel grassroots marketing beyond retail environments.

Is Street Teams Co more affordable than Interactions Marketing?

Street Teams Co provides custom-quoted rates scaled to your campaign. Interactions Marketing uses program-based pricing for enterprise clients with higher minimum commitments. For outdoor sampling, street teams, and event staffing, Street Teams Co offers more accessible and transparent pricing.

Does Interactions Marketing offer street team or guerrilla marketing?

No. Interactions Marketing focuses exclusively on in-store product demonstrations at warehouse clubs and select retail chains. Street team marketing, guerrilla campaigns, flyer distribution, and outdoor experiential activations are not services they provide.

Can I use both Interactions Marketing and Street Teams Co?

Yes. Many CPG brands use Interactions for in-store demos at warehouse clubs while deploying Street Teams Co for outdoor sampling, street teams, and event activations. This dual approach creates multiple consumer touchpoints that build awareness outside the store and drive conversion inside it.
