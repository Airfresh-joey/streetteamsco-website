BDS Marketing vs Street Teams Co | 2026 Comparison

BDS Marketing (BDS Connected Solutions) specializes in retail product demos and in-store technology sales. Street Teams Co provides full-service street team marketing across 1,000+ cities. This comparison helps you decide which agency fits your campaign needs.

Introduction: Two Different Approaches to Brand Marketing

BDS Marketing, now operating as BDS Connected Solutions, is one of the largest retail marketing agencies in the United States. Founded in 1984, BDS has built its reputation on in-store product demonstrations, retail merchandising, assisted selling programs, and technology sales at major retail chains including Best Buy, Costco, Walmart, and Target. Their strength lies in the retail environment: getting products off shelves and into shopping carts through trained in-store staff. Street Teams Co takes a different approach. As a full-service grassroots marketing agency, Street Teams Co deploys brand ambassadors, street teams, and promotional staff across 1,000+ US cities for activations that happen on sidewalks, at events, in public spaces, at festivals, around convention centers, and yes, in retail locations too. The fundamental difference is scope: BDS is a retail specialist; Street Teams Co is a versatile street-level marketing partner. Understanding which model fits your campaign determines which agency will deliver the strongest ROI.

Services and Capabilities

BDS Marketing has built a powerful retail marketing machine. Their core services include in-store product demonstrations, retail merchandising and planogram compliance, assisted selling where trained brand advocates work alongside retail floor staff, technology sales programs, and consumer experience zones within retail environments. BDS excels when the goal is to move product at the point of sale. Their deep relationships with retail chains give them access to store management, dedicated demo space, and retail-specific analytics including sell-through rates and POS data integration.

Street Teams Co operates across the full grassroots marketing spectrum. Their eight service categories, street teams, brand ambassadors, event staffing, product sampling, guerrilla marketing, flyer distribution, experiential marketing, and promotional staffing, cover both indoor and outdoor environments. This versatility matters because effective product launches and brand campaigns rarely happen in a single channel. A CPG brand launching a new snack might sample at grocery stores (where BDS excels) and simultaneously deploy street teams at nearby parks, gyms, and farmer's markets (where Street Teams Co excels). An integrated approach reaches consumers at multiple touchpoints throughout their day, reinforcing brand awareness and driving both immediate trial and retail purchase.

Pricing and Accessibility

BDS Marketing operates primarily at the enterprise level. Their programs are designed for major consumer brands and technology companies running national retail programs across hundreds or thousands of store locations. Pricing is custom and enterprise-scale, with program minimums that typically start at $50,000 and can scale into the millions for national rollouts. This pricing model serves their target clients well but puts BDS out of reach for mid-market brands, startups, and companies testing grassroots marketing for the first time.

Street Teams Co offers dramatically more accessible pricing. Every campaign is custom-quoted to your goals, team size, and market, with low minimums and flexible single-city options. Technology and Campaign Reporting

BDS Marketing provides retail-focused analytics tied to in-store performance, including sell-through rates, demo engagement counts, and POS data where available through retailer partnerships. Their reporting is built for the retail channel and integrates with the metrics that retail marketing managers track: units sold, demo-to-sale conversion rates, and program compliance across store locations.

Street Teams Co provides a different type of campaign intelligence through their real-time analytics dashboard. Every field campaign includes live GPS tracking of all staff, timestamped and geotagged photo documentation, consumer engagement counts, distribution metrics, lead capture data, and post-campaign analytics. This reporting is designed for grassroots marketing where success metrics include impressions generated, samples distributed, leads captured, social media mentions, and geographic coverage verified. For brands running outdoor activations, event staffing, or street team campaigns, this real-time visibility provides accountability and optimization capabilities that are not available from retail-focused reporting systems.

Geographic Coverage and Flexibility

BDS Marketing's coverage is tied to retail locations. They can staff product demonstrations at Best Buy, Costco, Walmart, and Target locations nationwide, which provides substantial geographic reach within the retail channel. However, their coverage does not extend to non-retail environments: outdoor venues, event spaces, convention centers, college campuses, public parks, or urban street locations.

Street Teams Co maintains active staff networks in over 1,000 US cities across all 50 states, deployable to any venue type. Whether you need brand ambassadors at a trade show in Las Vegas, street teams on the Venice Beach boardwalk, product sampling at a farmer's market in Portland, or promotional staff at a music festival in Austin, Street Teams Co's geographic coverage and venue flexibility accommodate virtually any activation concept. This breadth is particularly valuable for brands whose marketing strategies combine retail presence with experiential and street-level tactics.

When to Choose BDS Marketing

BDS Marketing is the right partner when your primary objective is driving in-store sales at major retail chains. If you are launching a consumer electronics product at Best Buy, running a food sampling program at Costco, or deploying retail advocates across Walmart locations, BDS brings unmatched retail-specific expertise, retailer relationships, and point-of-sale analytics. Their scale in the retail channel, with thousands of trained demonstrators across national retail footprints, is difficult to replicate. For enterprise brands with dedicated retail marketing budgets exceeding $50,000 and goals centered on sell-through metrics, BDS is a strong choice.

When to Choose Street Teams Co

Street Teams Co is the better choice when your campaign extends beyond the retail floor, when you need versatile multi-channel grassroots marketing, or when your budget and timeline require flexibility. Choose Street Teams Co when:

  • You need street-level activations: Street teams, guerrilla marketing, and outdoor experiential are core competencies that BDS does not offer
  • Your campaign is multi-channel: Combining retail sampling with street teams, event staffing, and guerrilla tactics under one agency
  • Your budget is below enterprise thresholds: Flexible low-minimum campaigns with transparent custom quotes
  • You need rapid deployment: 48-hour rush staffing for time-sensitive opportunities
  • You value real-time field accountability: GPS tracking and live dashboards during every activation
  • You are staffing events, not retail: Trade shows, festivals, conferences, and brand events are Street Teams Co's territory

For brands that want to surround retail presence with street-level marketing, or those whose grassroots strategy extends beyond the four walls of a store, Street Teams Co provides the versatility, accessibility, and nationwide coverage to execute effectively. For in-store sampling combined with outdoor activations, street teams, or event staffing, Street Teams Co is the better integrated option.
