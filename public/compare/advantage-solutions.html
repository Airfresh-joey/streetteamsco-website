Advantage Solutions vs Street Teams Co: Full Comparison 2026

A thorough comparison of a large CPG services corporation and an agile, nationwide street team marketing agency. We compare product sampling, demos, staffing, pricing, flexibility, and technology to help you select the right partner for your brand.

Overview: Advantage Solutions and Street Teams Co

Advantage Solutions and Street Teams Co both help brands connect with consumers through face-to-face marketing, but they operate at fundamentally different scales and with different service philosophies. Advantage Solutions (NASDAQ: ADV) is a publicly traded, multi-billion-dollar company that provides a broad range of services to consumer packaged goods manufacturers and retailers, including in-store product demonstrations, retail merchandising, shopper marketing, analytics, and sales support. They are one of the largest players in the retail services industry, operating through subsidiaries and divisions that serve enterprise CPG brands at massive national scale. Their Clubhouse sampling division alone runs hundreds of thousands of product demonstrations annually across major retail chains.

Street Teams Co is an independent street team marketing agency that provides eight categories of grassroots marketing services across 1,000+ US cities. While dramatically smaller in corporate size, Street Teams Co offers advantages that enterprise clients and mid-market brands alike find compelling: transparent published pricing, real-time GPS campaign tracking, a Show-Up Guarantee, no minimum spending requirements, and rapid deployment capabilities. Their services extend well beyond the retail environment to include street team activations, guerrilla marketing, event staffing, and experiential marketing in diverse venues and settings.

This comparison is particularly relevant for brands evaluating whether a large corporate provider or an agile independent agency is the better fit for their product sampling, brand ambassador, and field marketing needs. We present the facts fairly to help you make the right choice.

Detailed Analysis: Services and Specialization

Advantage Solutions Services

Advantage Solutions operates through multiple divisions serving the CPG industry. Their consumer experiences division runs in-store product demonstrations at retailers including Costco, Sam's Club, Walmart, and regional grocery chains. Their retail merchandising division provides shelf stocking, planogram compliance, display installation, and retail audit services. Additional divisions handle shopper marketing, digital commerce support, retailer analytics, and sales brokerage. The breadth of their services within the retail ecosystem is unmatched. For a large CPG brand that needs in-store demos in 10,000 locations, shelf resets across a national retail chain, and point-of-sale data analysis to measure impact, Advantage Solutions provides an integrated corporate solution at a scale that few companies can match.

Street Teams Co Services

Street Teams Co serves brands across a wider variety of marketing environments with eight distinct service categories: street team activations, brand ambassadors, event staffing, product sampling, guerrilla marketing, flyer distribution, experiential marketing, and promotional staffing. While product sampling is a core offering, Street Teams Co deploys sampling teams in environments that Advantage Solutions typically does not service: outdoor festivals, farmers markets, sporting events, college campuses, concert venues, fitness centers, office buildings, and public streets. This versatility allows brands to sample products where their target consumers actually spend time, not just where they shop. For brands that want product sampling alongside other field marketing tactics like brand ambassadors at events, street teams in downtown areas, and guerrilla activations on social media-worthy backdrops, Street Teams Co combines it all under one roof.

Detailed Analysis: Pricing, Flexibility, and Accessibility

The accessibility gap between these two agencies is among the most important factors for many brands. Advantage Solutions operates with enterprise sales cycles, corporate procurement processes, and minimum campaign commitments that reflect their Fortune 500 client base. Getting started typically requires formal RFP processes, multi-week proposal timelines, and budget commitments that can be prohibitive for emerging brands, mid-size companies, or agencies managing smaller campaign budgets. Their pricing structures are not publicly available and are negotiated as part of longer-term contracts.

Street Teams Co takes the opposite approach to accessibility. Published rate ranges are available on their pricing page: product sampling staff at $25-$45/hr, brand ambassadors at $25-$45/hr, event staff at $25-$55/hr, and street team members at $25-$50/hr. There are no minimum campaign requirements, meaning a brand can start with a single-city, single-day sampling activation to test the partnership before scaling. Proposals are delivered within 24-48 hours, and campaigns can launch within 48 hours in major markets. Every proposal fully itemizes costs. This speed and transparency make Street Teams Co accessible to brands at every stage of growth, from a startup sampling their product at a local farmers market to a national brand running a 50-city product launch.

Detailed Analysis: Technology Comparison

Both agencies use technology, but for different purposes. Advantage Solutions has built extensive retail analytics capabilities including point-of-sale data tracking, sales lift measurement, shopper behavior analysis, and retail compliance auditing. These tools help enterprise CPG brands understand how their in-store programs impact retail sales and shopper purchasing patterns. The data is invaluable for brands optimizing retail marketing spend at scale.

Street Teams Co's technology platform focuses on field operations visibility. Real-time GPS tracking confirms the location of every sampling team member, event staffer, and brand ambassador during active campaigns. Timestamped, geotagged photos document every touchpoint. Live counters track samples distributed, consumers engaged, leads captured, and materials distributed. The client-accessible dashboard consolidates everything in one view. For product sampling specifically, this means a brand can verify in real time that their sampling team is at the target location, see photos of the setup and consumer interactions, and track exactly how many samples have been distributed hour by hour. This operational transparency is a different value proposition than retail sales analytics, but it directly addresses the fundamental accountability question in field marketing: is my team performing as promised?

Who Should Choose Advantage Solutions

Advantage Solutions is the right choice for large CPG brands that need massive-scale in-store retail programs. If your primary need is tens of thousands of product demonstrations across Costco, Sam's Club, Walmart, or grocery chains, combined with retail merchandising, planogram compliance, and point-of-sale analytics, Advantage Solutions has the infrastructure and retailer relationships to deliver at that scale. Their integrated retail services model means a single vendor can handle sampling, merchandising, shelf management, and analytics across your entire retail distribution footprint. Brands with annual field marketing budgets exceeding seven figures and programs requiring year-round retail execution at national chain level will benefit most from Advantage Solutions' capabilities and corporate resources.

Who Should Choose Street Teams Co

Street Teams Co is the better choice for brands of any size that need flexible, transparent, and versatile field marketing that extends beyond the retail shelf. Whether you need product sampling at festivals, brand ambassadors at trade shows, street teams distributing in urban areas, guerrilla marketing at concerts, or event staffing for corporate functions, Street Teams Co handles it all with published rates starting at $25/hr and no minimum commitments. The real-time GPS tracking and Show-Up Guarantee provide accountability that even Advantage Solutions' enterprise clients rarely experience at the field level. For emerging brands running their first sampling program, for mid-size companies that find enterprise vendor procurement too cumbersome, and for established brands that want to complement their in-store programs with out-of-store field marketing, Street Teams Co delivers professional execution with modern transparency and agility.
