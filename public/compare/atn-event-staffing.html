Coverage and Reach

Geographic coverage is a decisive differentiator between these two agencies. Street Teams Co maintains active, vetted staff networks in over 1,000 cities across all 50 states. This means brands can launch simultaneous campaigns in Los Angeles, Miami, Denver, and Minneapolis with local staff in each market, all managed through a single account relationship. Every major convention city, college town, and mid-size market is covered, which is critical for brands running national product launches, multi-city sampling tours, or trade show circuits. ATN Event Staffing serves major metropolitan markets with a core focus on cities where trade shows and large brand events are concentrated. Their coverage is strong in markets like Las Vegas, New York, Los Angeles, and Miami. However, for campaigns extending beyond the top 20 metros or requiring simultaneous deployment in 10+ cities, ATN's geographic footprint may require supplemental partnerships. For single-city activations in a major metro, both agencies can deliver. For national programs, Street Teams Co's infrastructure provides a clear logistical advantage.

Pricing and Transparency

Pricing structure and transparency differ significantly between the two agencies. Street Teams Co publishes rate ranges on their pricing page: brand ambassadors start at $25-$45/hr, promotional models at $35-$65/hr, event staff at $25-$55/hr, and street team members at $25-$50/hr. Every proposal from Street Teams Co itemizes staff rates, management fees, travel costs, and material charges so brands can see exactly where their budget is allocated. ATN Event Staffing uses custom, project-based quoting without published rate cards. This approach is common among event staffing agencies and allows pricing to be tailored to campaign complexity, but it makes upfront comparison more difficult. Brands requesting proposals from both agencies should ask for itemized breakdowns to make fair comparisons. For organizations with procurement processes that require standardized rate cards or transparent unit costs, Street Teams Co's published pricing simplifies vendor evaluation and approval.

Services Offered

ATN Event Staffing specializes in event staffing and brand ambassador placement. Their core services include trade show booth staffing, promotional models for brand activations, brand ambassadors for experiential campaigns, and event registration and hosting staff. They serve their core market well and have deep experience in the event staffing vertical. Street Teams Co offers eight distinct service categories that span the full grassroots marketing spectrum: street team activations, brand ambassadors, event staffing, product sampling, guerrilla marketing, flyer distribution, experiential marketing, and promotional staffing. This breadth matters when brands want to surround a trade show with additional marketing activities, such as deploying street teams around the convention center, running a product sampling program at nearby retail locations, or executing a guerrilla activation to generate buzz. One agency handling all of these eliminates coordination overhead and ensures consistent brand messaging across every touchpoint.

Quality and Training

Both agencies take staff quality seriously, but their approaches reflect their different service models. ATN Event Staffing curates their talent pool for event environments, selecting staff with strong presentation skills, professional appearance, and experience working trade show floors, corporate galas, and brand activations. Their deep focus on event settings means their talent is well-prepared for the specific demands of booth staffing, product demonstrations, and VIP hospitality. Street Teams Co applies a structured vetting and training process across all eight service categories. Every staff member undergoes background checks, communication assessments, and scenario-based evaluations before joining the active roster. Campaign-specific training programs cover product knowledge, brand messaging, engagement techniques, and reporting requirements. A performance rating system tracks each team member's reliability, quality, and client feedback across assignments. The Show-Up Guarantee adds a contractual accountability layer: if a confirmed staff member does not arrive, Street Teams Co provides a replacement or credits the client. This guarantee directly addresses the number one complaint in the promotional staffing industry.

Technology and Reporting

Campaign reporting technology represents one of the largest gaps between the two agencies. Street Teams Co provides every client with access to a real-time analytics dashboard that includes live GPS tracking of all field staff, timestamped and geotagged photo uploads, consumer engagement counts, distribution metrics, and lead capture data. Clients can log in at any point during an active campaign and see exactly where their teams are, what they are doing, and how the campaign is performing. This real-time visibility enables mid-campaign adjustments, immediate issue resolution, and verifiable accountability. ATN Event Staffing provides post-campaign reporting that includes event summaries, photos, and performance metrics. Their reporting is professional and adequate for many campaign types, but it does not include the real-time tracking capability that allows clients to monitor operations as they happen. For brands that manage large budgets, report to executive stakeholders, or need to verify field operations across multiple markets simultaneously, Street Teams Co's technology infrastructure offers a meaningful upgrade in transparency and control.
