George P Johnson vs Street Teams Co | Agency Comparison

GPJ is one of the world's largest experiential marketing agencies serving Fortune 500 brands with massive produced experiences. Street Teams Co is a nationwide grassroots marketing agency with accessible pricing and 1,000+ city coverage. This comparison helps brands at every budget level find the right fit.

Introduction: Enterprise Experiential vs. Accessible Grassroots

George P Johnson Experience Marketing (GPJ), a subsidiary of Project Worldwide, is one of the most established and recognized experiential marketing agencies globally. Founded in 1914, GPJ has over a century of experience producing large-scale brand experiences for Fortune 500 clients including IBM, Google, Samsung, Cisco, and Microsoft. Their work spans massive trade show exhibits, corporate event productions, brand experience centers, product launch spectacles, and immersive digital-physical activations. GPJ operates at the highest end of the experiential spectrum, with project budgets that typically start at $250,000 and can reach into the tens of millions for flagship programs.

Street Teams Co occupies a fundamentally different position in the marketing agency ecosystem. As a nationwide grassroots marketing agency, Street Teams Co provides the boots-on-the-ground workforce that powers street team activations, brand ambassador deployments, product sampling campaigns, event staffing, guerrilla marketing, and experiential activations across 1,000+ US cities. Pricing is custom-quoted and scaled to your campaign. This comparison is not about declaring one agency better than the other. It is about understanding which agency model fits your campaign objectives, budget, and marketing philosophy.

Services and Capabilities

GPJ's services are built for spectacle and impact at the highest level. They concept, design, fabricate, and produce brand experiences that make headlines. A GPJ project might involve transforming a 50,000-square-foot convention hall into an immersive brand world, building a custom product launch venue from the ground up, or creating a multi-sensory experience center that lives for months. Their capabilities span creative strategy, spatial design, content production, digital experience platforms, fabrication and logistics, on-site production management, and post-event analytics. For brands that need a produced experience that generates earned media, executive-level engagement, and category-defining moments, GPJ delivers at a level that few agencies can match.

Street Teams Co's capabilities are built for reach, frequency, and scalable consumer engagement. Eight service categories cover the full grassroots marketing playbook: street team activations, brand ambassadors, event staffing, product sampling, guerrilla marketing, flyer distribution, experiential marketing, and promotional staffing. These services put trained brand representatives in direct contact with consumers at events, on streets, at retail locations, at festivals, and in communities across 1,000+ cities. Where GPJ creates a single extraordinary moment, Street Teams Co creates thousands of authentic consumer interactions across multiple markets simultaneously.

Pricing and Accessibility

The pricing gap between these two agencies reflects their fundamentally different service models and target markets. GPJ's project budgets typically begin at $250,000 for smaller engagements and scale to $1 million, $5 million, or more for flagship experiences. These budgets encompass creative development, design, fabrication, technology, production management, logistics, and on-site execution. GPJ's clients are enterprise organizations with dedicated experiential marketing budgets supported by corporate marketing, brand, and events teams.

Street Teams Co makes professional grassroots marketing accessible to brands at every budget level. Pricing is custom-quoted for brand ambassadors and street team staff, with complete, custom-quoted campaign packages. A startup launching its first product sampling campaign and a Fortune 500 company deploying brand ambassadors across 50 cities both receive the same transparent, itemized pricing. This accessibility means that brands priced out of enterprise experiential agencies can still execute professional, accountable grassroots marketing programs that drive measurable results.

Speed and Agility

GPJ projects require extensive planning timelines. A major trade show exhibit might begin concepting 12 to 18 months before the event. A product launch experience requires 6 to 12 months of development. This timeline reflects the complexity of designing, fabricating, and producing high-end experiences. It is not a weakness but rather a requirement of delivering at GPJ's quality level.

Street Teams Co operates at a fundamentally different speed. Standard campaign deployment takes 2 to 4 weeks from inquiry to execution. Rush staffing is available within 48 hours in major metros. This agility means brands can respond to market opportunities, competitive moves, cultural moments, or unexpected events with field activations that would be impossible to organize through a production-focused agency. A CPG brand that learns its competitor is sampling at a music festival next weekend can have Street Teams Co deploy a counter-sampling team within days.

The Complementary Approach

The most sophisticated marketing organizations do not choose between enterprise experiential and grassroots marketing. They integrate both into a comprehensive strategy. GPJ creates the tentpole brand moment, the flagship experience at CES that generates 50 million media impressions. Street Teams Co extends that moment by deploying street teams around the convention center, running product sampling at hotels and restaurants where attendees gather, and staffing satellite events throughout the host city. The tentpole creates the headline; the grassroots execution creates the reach. Some brands use GPJ for creative concepting and production while engaging Street Teams Co for nationwide field staffing, combining GPJ's design excellence with Street Teams Co's operational footprint and reliability guarantees.

When to Choose George P Johnson

Choose GPJ when your objective is creating a landmark brand experience that generates massive earned media, engages executive-level stakeholders, and positions your brand as a category leader. GPJ is the right partner for trade show exhibits at CES, MWC, or NRF; corporate event productions; product launch spectacles; brand experience centers; and any activation where production design, creative excellence, and immersive technology are the primary success factors. If your budget starts at $250,000+ and your goal is a single extraordinary brand moment, GPJ brings world-class capability.

When to Choose Street Teams Co

Choose Street Teams Co when your marketing strategy requires consumer reach, frequency, multi-city scale, or budget accessibility. Street Teams Co is the right partner when:

  • Speed matters: 48-hour rush staffing versus months of production planning
  • You need boots on the ground: Street teams, brand ambassadors, samplers, and event staff in the field
  • Ongoing programs: Weekly, monthly, or quarterly campaigns that require sustained field presence
  • Surrounding a major event: Extending a flagship experience with street-level activations around the venue
  • Measurable grassroots tactics: Product samples distributed, leads captured, flyers handed out, and consumers engaged, all tracked in real time
