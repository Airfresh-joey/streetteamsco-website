Whether you are planning a single-city guerrilla activation or a 50-city product launch, this side-by-side analysis will help you identify which agency aligns best with your campaign goals and budget.

Coverage and Reach

Geographic coverage is one of the most significant differences between these two agencies. Street Teams Co maintains active staff networks in over 1,000 cities across all 50 states, from major metros like New York, Los Angeles, and Chicago to mid-size markets like Raleigh, Boise, and Albuquerque. This infrastructure enables simultaneous multi-city campaigns with local staff who understand each market's demographics, regulations, and high-traffic locations. Alt Terrain's operations center on the East Coast, with their strongest presence in Boston, New York, and a handful of additional metros. They have managed select national projects, but their core geographic strength lies in the northeastern United States. For brands planning campaigns limited to Boston or New York, Alt Terrain's local market knowledge is an asset. For brands requiring coverage across five, ten, or fifty cities, Street Teams Co's nationwide infrastructure eliminates the need to coordinate with multiple regional providers.

Pricing and Transparency

Street Teams Co provides transparent, custom-quoted pricing depending on the role and market, and every proposal itemizes staff rates, management fees, travel costs, and materials. This transparency means brands can build accurate budgets when requesting a free quote and compare proposals on a clear, apples-to-apples basis. Alt Terrain uses a custom project-based pricing model, which is standard in the creative agency space but makes it harder to estimate costs upfront. Alt Terrain's project pricing may include creative development fees, production costs, and media placement charges alongside staffing, which reflects the integrated nature of their campaigns. For brands that prioritize cost predictability and want to understand exactly what they are paying for each line item, Street Teams Co's itemized custom-quote approach offers a simpler evaluation process. For brands that want a fully integrated creative-plus-execution package and are less concerned with hourly rate transparency, Alt Terrain's project-based approach may work well.

Services Offered

Alt Terrain has carved out a specialized niche in guerrilla marketing and alternative advertising. Their service portfolio includes wild posting, wheat-pasting, street projections, sidewalk stencils, street teams, and other unconventional media channels. This specialization gives them deep expertise in creative guerrilla tactics that generate earned media and social buzz. Street Teams Co provides eight distinct service categories: street teams, brand ambassadors, event staffing, product sampling, guerrilla marketing, experiential marketing, promotional staffing, and flyer distribution. This breadth means a single agency can handle a brand's entire grassroots marketing program, from product sampling at Whole Foods to guerrilla activations at SXSW to brand ambassador staffing at CES. Brands that need multiple service types benefit from the consolidated account management and consistent quality that comes from a single-agency relationship.

Quality and Training

Alt Terrain's staff are typically embedded within their creative campaign framework, meaning they receive training specific to each guerrilla concept and understand the nuances of unconventional marketing tactics. Their 15+ years of experience in the guerrilla space has produced a team that knows how to execute wild posting campaigns, manage street projections, and navigate the logistical challenges of alternative advertising. Street Teams Co vets every staff member through background checks, communication assessments, and role-play scenarios before they join the active roster. Each campaign includes custom brand training with product knowledge testing, and a performance rating system tracks reliability and quality across assignments. Their Show-Up Guarantee provides an additional accountability layer, ensuring that staffing commitments are met or the client is compensated. Both agencies maintain professional quality standards, but their training approaches reflect their different specializations: Alt Terrain trains for creative guerrilla execution, while Street Teams Co trains for versatile, scalable field marketing across multiple campaign types.

Technology and Reporting

Technology is a clear differentiator in this comparison. Street Teams Co provides a real-time reporting dashboard that includes GPS tracking of every team member, timestamped photo uploads from the field, live engagement metrics, and post-campaign analytics. Clients can monitor their campaign in real time from any device, see exactly where their teams are, and view consumer interactions as they happen. This level of visibility is particularly valuable for multi-city campaigns where direct oversight is impossible. Alt Terrain provides post-campaign reporting that includes campaign documentation, photo assets, and results summaries. Their reporting is thorough for the guerrilla marketing context but does not include the real-time tracking and live data feeds that Street Teams Co offers. For brands that want minute-by-minute campaign visibility and the ability to make real-time adjustments, Street Teams Co's technology platform is a significant advantage.

Client Support

Street Teams Co assigns a dedicated account manager to every campaign, providing a single point of contact from initial planning through post-campaign analysis. Their 94% client retention rate suggests strong ongoing relationships and consistent service delivery. Rush staffing in 48 hours is available for urgent needs, and their team handles all logistics including permits, materials, and branded attire coordination. Alt Terrain brings a consultative, creative-agency approach to client relationships. Their team works closely with brands on campaign concepting and creative development, investing time in understanding brand positioning and target audiences. For brands that value creative partnership and campaign ideation, Alt Terrain's approach delivers genuine strategic input. The support models reflect different agency philosophies: Street Teams Co emphasizes operational reliability and responsiveness, while Alt Terrain emphasizes creative collaboration and strategic thinking.

The real-time reporting dashboard gives you visibility into every field operation as it happens.
