About Alt Terrain

Alt Terrain is a Boston-headquartered guerrilla marketing and alternative advertising agency that has been in operation for over 15 years. They specialize in unconventional marketing campaigns including wild posting, street teams, projections, and creative street-level activations. Alt Terrain has developed a strong reputation for creative campaign development, particularly for entertainment, lifestyle, and consumer brands in major East Coast markets.

Alt Terrain's strengths include their creative campaign ideation, deep experience with alternative media channels like wild posting and wheat-pasting, and a portfolio of work for recognizable entertainment and consumer brands.

About Street Teams Co

Street Teams Co is a nationwide street team marketing agency with a broader service offering and geographic reach. With street team activations, brand ambassadors, event staffing, product sampling, guerrilla marketing, and more, Street Teams Co handles every type of grassroots marketing campaign across 1,000+ cities.

Key Differences

For brands needing consistent execution across 10, 20, or 50+ cities, Street Teams Co provides more comprehensive geographic reach.

Service Breadth

Alt Terrain focuses primarily on guerrilla marketing, wild posting, and alternative advertising channels. Street Teams Co provides eight distinct service categories spanning the entire grassroots marketing spectrum. If you need a single agency partner to handle street teams, product sampling, event staffing, and guerrilla activations, Street Teams Co offers that consolidated approach.

Creative Approach

Alt Terrain has built a strong creative portfolio over 15+ years in the guerrilla marketing space. Street Teams Co combines creative campaign strategy with operational scale, focusing on campaigns that are both creative and scalable across multiple markets.

Pricing Model

Street Teams Co publishes transparent pricing starting at $25/hr with itemized proposals. Alt Terrain works on a custom project-based pricing model. For budget-conscious brands or those comparing multiple agencies, Street Teams Co's transparent approach simplifies the evaluation process.

Who Should Choose Which?

Choose Street Teams Co if: You need nationwide coverage, multiple service types from one agency, transparent pricing, real-time campaign tracking, and fast deployment. Best for multi-city campaigns, product sampling, event staffing, and comprehensive grassroots marketing programs.
