About Kinetic Events

Kinetic Events is an experiential marketing and event production agency that creates branded experiences and live activations. They specialize in designing and producing immersive brand environments, pop-up experiences, and large-scale event activations for consumer and corporate brands.

Kinetic Events brings strong creative and production capabilities to experiential marketing campaigns. Their focus on full-service event production includes concept design, fabrication, venue procurement, logistics, and on-site management for experiential activations.

About Street Teams Co

Street Teams Co is a nationwide street team marketing and brand ambassador agency that combines experiential marketing with grassroots field marketing capabilities. In addition to experiential marketing, Street Teams Co provides street team activations, product sampling, event staffing, guerrilla marketing, and more across 1,000+ cities.

Key Differences

Production vs. Field Marketing

Kinetic Events excels at designing and producing experiential environments and branded installations. Street Teams Co focuses on deploying trained staff for face-to-face marketing campaigns at scale. If your primary need is an immersive pop-up experience with custom fabrication, Kinetic Events brings dedicated production expertise. If you need brand ambassadors, street teams, and sampling staff deployed across multiple cities, Street Teams Co provides that operational scale.

Scale and Coverage

Street Teams Co's 1,000+ city coverage and 10,000+ staff network enables rapid deployment across any US market. Kinetic Events operates in select markets with a project-based approach. For campaigns requiring consistent nationwide execution, Street Teams Co provides broader reach.

Budget Flexibility

Street Teams Co offers campaigns starting at $25/hr for street team staff, making grassroots marketing accessible at various budget levels. Kinetic Events' experiential productions typically require larger budgets given the fabrication and production components. Both agencies deliver strong ROI at their respective price points.

Who Should Choose Which?

Choose Street Teams Co if: You need nationwide street team deployments, brand ambassador staffing, product sampling, or guerrilla marketing with transparent pricing and real-time tracking.

Consider Kinetic Events if: You need a large-scale experiential production with custom fabrication and full event design in a single market.
