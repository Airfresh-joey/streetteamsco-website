Street Teams Co vs Advantage Solutions for Retail In-Store Sampling

A fair, vertical-specific comparison for CPG marketers running retail in-store sampling and product demos. We compare retail network scale, GPS-verified reliability, measurement, deployment speed, and pricing transparency so you can pick the right partner.

Introduction

Advantage Solutions and Street Teams Co both work in retail in-store sampling, but they operate at fundamentally different scales and within different parts of the retail ecosystem. Advantage Solutions is one of the largest retail sales and marketing services companies in the United States, with exclusive and preferred partnerships at major retailers including Walmart, Target, Costco, Sam's Club, Kroger, and others. Their scale, retailer relationships, and infrastructure are genuinely unmatched for retailer-program execution at those banners. Street Teams Co is a faster, more customizable in-store demo staffing and retail sampling partner built for branded campaigns where GPS-verified shifts, transparent pricing, and measurement matter more than network depth. This comparison is written for CPG brand managers, trade marketing leads, and shopper marketing teams weighing the two approaches.

Two Different Retail Worlds

To choose intelligently between these agencies, it helps to understand that "retail in-store sampling" actually covers two related but distinct worlds. The first is the retailer-managed demo program, typified by the Costco roadshow program or the Walmart-aligned sampling networks. These programs operate under the retailer's preferred vendor relationships, follow the retailer's standards, and have access advantages that outside agencies simply cannot replicate. Advantage Solutions is one of the dominant players in this world. The second is the brand-managed sampling program: a CPG brand designs the campaign, selects the stores and windows, and runs the activation as a branded program with its own standards, measurement, and creative. Both Advantage and Street Teams Co operate in this second world, but Street Teams Co was built specifically for it. Knowing which world your program lives in is the first step in vendor selection.

When Advantage Solutions Is the Right Choice

Advantage Solutions is the right choice when your sampling program lives inside a retailer-managed program at one of the major banners where they hold exclusive or preferred relationships. If you are running a Costco roadshow, a Sam's Club demo program, a Walmart-aligned shopper marketing initiative, or a Kroger-funded sampling activation, the retailer's preferred vendor list often dictates the partner choice, and Advantage is frequently the vendor. Their scale, infrastructure, established compliance, and tenure with each retailer's program management team give them legitimate operational advantages in that environment. For mass retailer programs where the retailer is dictating the standards and the funding partially or fully comes from co-op dollars, working with the retailer's preferred partner is usually the path of least resistance. Their massive footprint also means they can support truly enormous programs that span thousands of stores simultaneously, which is a scale very few agencies can match.

When Street Teams Co Is the Right Choice

Street Teams Co is the right choice for branded sampling programs that operate outside the major retailer-exclusive networks, for custom multi-banner campaigns, for new SKU launches with tight ramp windows, and for any program where the brand wants strong measurement and accountability standards. The model is built around four characteristics that matter for branded retail sampling: GPS-verified shift accountability backed by the Show-Up Guarantee, syndicated data alignment with SPINS, IRI, and Circana so brands can correlate sampling activity with velocity lift, transparent published pricing on the pricing page so brand managers can model program economics, and fast ramp with 48-72 hour windows in major metros and 7-10 day national rollouts. This combination is particularly strong for emerging and mid-size CPG brands, for new SKU launches, and for programs at natural, specialty, convenience, ethnic, or independent grocery channels outside the major exclusives. Brands looking for an alternative to Advantage Solutions for branded measurement-driven sampling typically find that Street Teams Co fits the brief.

Reliability and GPS Verification

The honest reality of mass retail demo networks is that show-up rates vary by banner, geography, day-part, and shift type. Programs operated at massive scale make show-up consistency operationally challenging in a way that smaller, more carefully managed rosters can avoid. Advantage Solutions and other large operators work hard to maintain reliability, and their performance is generally acceptable for most retailer-program work. For brands running branded programs where each missed store-shift represents a real loss in trial volume and end-cap ROI, however, the variability is a meaningful operational risk. Street Teams Co addresses this directly with GPS verification on every shift, real-time visibility for clients via a live dashboard, and the Show-Up Guarantee that contractually commits to providing a replacement or credit if a confirmed staff member fails to arrive. The GPS data does double duty: it gives the client real-time accountability during the campaign and produces a verifiable record for post-campaign measurement.

Measurement: Tying Sampling to Velocity Lift

Modern CPG brands measure sampling success against scan velocity and trial conversion in the relevant retailer's syndicated data. Street Teams Co aligns every sampling campaign with SPINS, IRI, and Circana so brands can correlate sampling activity with velocity lift at the store, banner, and SKU level. Advantage Solutions offers similar measurement capabilities for programs where they manage the data integration, particularly within retailer-funded programs. For brands running branded sampling programs where they own the measurement loop, Street Teams Co's standard reporting workflow includes syndicated data correlation as a default rather than a custom build. The practical implication is that brand managers running their own measurement do not need to scope and budget syndicated data integration separately.

Pricing for Retail Sampling

Street Teams Co publishes rates on its pricing page: product demo specialists at $35-$65/hr, brand ambassadors at $25-$45/hr, and lead BAs at up to $75/hr. Branded sampling programs typically range from $10,000 to $50,000 per market for one to four-week programs and $100K-$300K for national multi-retailer rollouts inclusive of staff, branded materials, syndicated data correlation, and account management. Advantage Solutions operates through a mix of retailer-funded programs, co-op-funded shopper marketing engagements, and custom client work that varies widely by retailer and category. Direct pricing comparison is difficult because the scope and funding models differ, but for branded multi-banner programs where the brand controls the budget, Street Teams Co's published pricing is generally more cost-efficient than enterprise full-service alternatives.
