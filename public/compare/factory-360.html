Factory 360 vs Street Teams Co: Full Comparison 2026

A detailed, honest comparison of two experiential marketing agencies with different strengths. We break down services, pricing, geographic coverage, technology, and client experience to help you choose the right partner for your next campaign.

Overview: Factory 360 and Street Teams Co

Factory 360 and Street Teams Co both operate in the experiential and event marketing space, but they take fundamentally different approaches to helping brands connect with consumers. Factory 360 is a New York City-based experiential marketing agency known for producing creative, design-forward brand experiences including pop-up shops, immersive installations, and large-scale experiential activations. They have worked with major consumer brands on high-visibility campaigns that prioritize creative impact and social shareability. Their strength lies in concept development and production of premium brand moments in select markets.

Street Teams Co is a full-service street team marketing agency that focuses on scalable field execution across the entire United States. With active staff in 1,000+ cities, eight distinct service categories, and a technology platform that provides real-time campaign tracking, Street Teams Co is built for brands that need reliable grassroots marketing at scale. Whether the campaign involves brand ambassadors at a trade show, street teams distributing samples in downtown Denver, or guerrilla marketing activations surrounding a major festival, Street Teams Co handles the logistics, staffing, and reporting under one roof.

This comparison examines where each agency excels, where they differ, and which type of campaign is best served by each. We aim to be fair and factual so you can make an informed decision for your specific marketing objectives.

Detailed Analysis: Services

Factory 360 Services

Factory 360 positions itself as an experiential marketing agency with deep creative capabilities. Their service offerings center around brand experience design and production: pop-up retail environments, immersive brand installations, mobile tours, interactive product demonstrations, and large-scale event activations. They handle the full creative lifecycle from concept development through design, fabrication, logistics, and on-site execution. Their portfolio showcases visually striking campaigns for brands in consumer electronics, beverage, fashion, and entertainment. The agency excels when a brand needs a bespoke, high-impact experiential moment that generates press coverage and social media content.

Street Teams Co Services

Street Teams Co offers eight distinct service categories that cover the full grassroots marketing spectrum: street team activations, brand ambassadors, event staffing, product sampling, guerrilla marketing, flyer distribution, experiential marketing, and promotional staffing. This breadth enables brands to combine multiple tactics within a single campaign, for example deploying brand ambassadors inside a trade show while running street teams and product sampling activations in the surrounding neighborhood. The focus is on reliable, scalable field execution rather than creative production, making Street Teams Co the operational backbone for campaigns where the brand or its creative agency has already developed the concept.

Detailed Analysis: Pricing

Pricing models between these two agencies reflect their different value propositions. Factory 360 operates on custom project-based pricing that factors in creative development, design, fabrication, logistics, staffing, and production management. Because their campaigns involve significant creative and physical production, budgets tend to start higher and scale with complexity. This is standard for experiential agencies that deliver turnkey creative experiences, but it means smaller brands or campaigns with tighter budgets may find entry costs challenging.

Street Teams Co publishes rate ranges on their pricing page. Brand ambassadors start at $25-$45/hr, promotional models at $35-$65/hr, event staff at $25-$55/hr, and street team members at $25-$50/hr. Every proposal itemizes staff rates, management fees, travel costs, and material charges. This transparency makes budget planning straightforward and simplifies the procurement process for brands that need to compare multiple vendors or get internal approvals quickly. For campaigns focused on field execution, sampling, and staffing, Street Teams Co's pricing model delivers strong value per marketing dollar.

Detailed Analysis: Coverage

Geographic coverage is a significant differentiator. Factory 360 is headquartered in New York City and has its strongest presence in the Northeast, with the ability to execute in other major markets for specific projects. Their model involves deploying production teams to event locations, which works well for select high-budget activations but can add travel and logistics costs for campaigns outside their home market. Multi-city simultaneous deployments require significant advance planning and coordination.

Street Teams Co maintains active, pre-vetted staff networks in over 1,000 cities across all 50 states. This means a brand can launch a 20-city product sampling tour, staff a trade show in Las Vegas while running street teams in Chicago, or activate guerrilla marketing campaigns in college towns across the country, all through a single agency relationship. The local staff model eliminates travel costs for standard campaigns and enables rapid deployment. For brands running national programs, multi-city tours, or campaigns in markets outside the top 10 metros, Street Teams Co's infrastructure provides a clear logistical and cost advantage.

Detailed Analysis: Technology and Reporting

Street Teams Co provides every client with access to a real-time analytics dashboard that includes live GPS tracking of all field staff, timestamped and geotagged photo uploads, consumer engagement counts, distribution metrics, and lead capture data. Clients can monitor active campaigns from any device, make mid-campaign adjustments, and verify that staff are on location performing as expected. This level of transparency is rare in the field marketing industry and addresses a common concern among brand managers who cannot be physically present at every activation.

Factory 360 provides comprehensive post-campaign reporting that includes professional photography, video content, social media metrics, earned media value, and engagement data. Their reporting is geared toward demonstrating the creative impact and reach of experiential activations. While this is valuable for measuring campaign outcomes, it does not provide the real-time operational visibility that Street Teams Co's platform offers during active campaigns. For brands managing multiple simultaneous activations or those with strict compliance requirements, real-time tracking is a meaningful advantage.

Who Should Choose Factory 360

Factory 360 is the right choice when your primary need is creative experiential production. If you have a significant budget for a flagship brand experience, need an agency to develop the creative concept from scratch, and want a visually spectacular activation that generates press coverage and social sharing, Factory 360's design-led approach delivers. They are particularly strong for single-market, high-impact activations such as product launch pop-ups in New York, immersive brand installations at major events, or mobile tour experiences that require custom fabrication. Brands in fashion, luxury, technology, and entertainment that prioritize creative storytelling through physical experiences will find Factory 360's capabilities well-matched to their needs.

Who Should Choose Street Teams Co

Street Teams Co is the stronger choice when your campaign priorities are scalable execution, nationwide coverage, transparent pricing, and real-time accountability. If you need brand ambassadors in 15 cities simultaneously, a product sampling program across grocery store chains, street teams at festivals and sporting events, or event staffing at trade shows throughout the year, Street Teams Co's operational infrastructure handles it all. The published rate card, Show-Up Guarantee, and real-time GPS tracking give you full control over budget, reliability, and performance verification. With a 94% client retention rate and 500+ campaigns executed, Street Teams Co has proven its ability to deliver consistent, professional field marketing at scale. For brands that already have creative direction and need a dependable execution partner, or brands that need to stretch their marketing budgets across more markets and more touchpoints, Street Teams Co provides superior value.
