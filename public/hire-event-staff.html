Frequently Asked Questions About Hiring Event Staff

How fast can I hire event staff?

In major metro areas, we can deploy vetted, trained event staff within 48-72 hours for rush requests. Standard bookings with custom training and brand preparation require 2-3 weeks lead time. For large-scale events, multi-city activations, and peak seasons, we recommend booking 4-6 weeks in advance to guarantee your preferred talent and optimal team composition.

What is the minimum notice required to hire event staff?

Our minimum notice for rush event staffing is 48 hours in major US cities including New York, Los Angeles, Chicago, Miami, Dallas, Denver, Atlanta, and Las Vegas. For smaller markets and specialized roles (bilingual staff, TIPS-certified bartenders, technical demonstrators), we recommend at least one week of lead time. Standard bookings with full brand training start at 2 weeks notice.

What cities can I hire event staff in?

Street Teams Co provides event staff in over 1,000 US cities across all 50 states. Our largest talent pools are in New York, Los Angeles, Chicago, Miami, Dallas, Houston, Denver, Atlanta, Las Vegas, San Francisco, Phoenix, Philadelphia, Boston, Orlando, and Nashville. We also staff events in suburban areas, resort towns, and rural locations for festivals, fairs, and touring activations. View all locations.

What is your cancellation policy for hired event staff?

Cancellations made 7 or more days before the event receive a full refund. Cancellations within 3-7 days are subject to a 25% cancellation fee. Cancellations within 72 hours incur a 50% fee. Day-of cancellations are billed at the full contracted rate. Rescheduling to a new date within 30 days is free with 72+ hours notice.

What is included in your event staffing rates?

Our hourly rates include talent sourcing and vetting, background checks, custom brand training, scheduling and logistics coordination, on-site field management, GPS time and attendance tracking, real-time photo reporting, and post-event performance summaries. Travel, parking, meals for shifts over 6 hours, branded uniforms, and specialized equipment are quoted separately based on event requirements.

Are your event staff W-2 employees or 1099 contractors?

Our standard staffing model uses 1099 independent contractors, which provides maximum flexibility for project-based event work, multi-market activations, and variable scheduling. If your company requires W-2 employees for compliance or policy reasons, we can accommodate that request with adjusted pricing. Contact us to discuss which model fits your needs.
