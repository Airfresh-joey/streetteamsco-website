Frequently Asked Questions: CES Staffing

How much does CES booth staffing cost?

CES staffing is custom-quoted based on the roles you need, headcount, show days, and hours. Booth hosts, technical product demonstrators, and multilingual staff each price differently, and CES week commands premium Vegas market rates. Tell us your booth size and goals and we will send a free itemized quote.

When should I book staff for CES 2027?

As early as possible. CES is the largest trade show in North America and the best Las Vegas talent is reserved months ahead. Booking by early fall gives you first pick of experienced CES veterans; rush placements during show week are possible but selection is limited.

What staffing roles do you provide for CES?

Booth hosts and greeters, product demonstrators trained on your tech, lead-capture specialists, crowd gatherers, presenters and emcees, registration support, interpreters and multilingual ambassadors, and off-floor street teams for Strip and downtown activations.

Do your staff handle CES exhibitor badges and compliance?

We coordinate exhibitor badge registration with your team and brief every staff member on CES and venue policies, union rules on the show floor, and your booth conduct standards before day one.

Can you provide multilingual staff for international visitors?

Yes. CES draws attendees from 150+ countries. We staff ambassadors fluent in Spanish, Mandarin, Korean, Japanese, French, German, and other languages so your booth converts international buyers, press, and distributors.

Can you run street teams outside the convention center during CES?

Yes. Some of the highest-ROI CES plays happen off the show floor: street teams along the Strip and Fremont Street, hotel and casino activations, party and lounge staffing, and rideshare-pickup-zone sampling. We handle Las Vegas permitting and venue permissions.
