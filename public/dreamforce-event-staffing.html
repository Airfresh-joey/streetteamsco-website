Frequently Asked Questions

How much does Dreamforce staffing cost?

Dreamforce staffing is custom-quoted based on roles, headcount, and days. San Francisco is a premium market during conference week, and polished B2B-ready talent books early. Send us your plan for a free itemized quote.

Can you staff activations outside Moscone Center?

Yes. Much of Dreamforce happens off-campus: hotel lounges, restaurant takeovers, rooftop events, and street-level brand moments in SoMa. We staff and manage all of it, with San Francisco permitting handled where needed.

When should I book Dreamforce staff?

Six to eight weeks out is comfortable; earlier is better for large teams or specialized roles. Conference-week demand in San Francisco is intense and the best B2B-experienced staff go first.

Do your staff have B2B and SaaS event experience?

Yes. We match Dreamforce placements to staff with conference and enterprise-audience experience, and every team completes your product and messaging training before the event.

Can staff work our booth and our off-site dinner program?

Yes. Many clients run one roster across expo hours and evening programs with shift rotations, one team lead, and consolidated reporting.
