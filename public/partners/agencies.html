How Agency Partnerships Work

Most agency partnerships follow one of three structures. We've built our operating model to support all three without renegotiation.

Sub-Vendor Engagement (Most Common)

You're the prime contractor with your client. Street Teams Co is your sub-vendor for field staff. You bill the client; we bill you. All compliance documentation (insurance, W-9, COIs, vendor MSA) is provided upfront. Net-30 standard, Net-15 with deposit.

Whitelabel Execution

Your client never knows we exist. We deploy under your brand, all field reports use your agency's logo and template, and our staff is briefed to introduce themselves as "[Your Agency] Brand Ambassadors." Common for agency-of-record relationships where the agency owns the brand interface.

Co-Branded Execution

Both agency and Street Teams Co are visible to the client. Common when the agency wants to demonstrate they're working with specialized execution partners. Branded reports show both logos; agency manages day-to-day; Street Teams Co handles staffing and field operations.

Agency-Friendly Pricing Standards

Standard rates: $25-$75/hr per BA (same as our published rates). Volume discounts: 10% off at 10+ staff, 15% off at 20+ staff, custom enterprise pricing at 50+. Agency program tier: after $50K in cumulative engagements, agencies qualify for a 5% partner discount on all future projects and priority deployment in tier-2 markets.

Frequently Asked Questions from Agency Partners

Will you sign our agency's MSA?

Yes. We've signed dozens of agency MSAs and rarely require redlines. Standard provisions (non-solicitation, confidentiality, IP, payment terms, insurance) all work for us out of the gate. We can usually return a signed MSA within 48 hours of receipt.

Can we get exclusive geographic territory?

We don't grant geographic exclusivity (we operate in 1,000+ cities and serve many agency partners across overlapping markets). However, we do guarantee strict non-solicitation of your specific named clients and we manage capacity conflicts so two agency clients with overlapping needs don't deplete the same local staff pool.

What's the smallest project you'll take from an agency?

Our minimum engagement is $1,500 (a single-day, single-city, 2-3 BA activation). For ongoing agency partnerships, we'll take projects below minimum if they're part of a programmatic relationship — e.g., monthly retainer agency clients with occasional small-market activations.

How do you handle creative direction?

We're an execution partner, not a creative agency. We don't propose creative concepts unsolicited. Your agency owns brand and creative; we own staffing, training, deployment, and reporting. If your client wants creative ideation, you handle that — we'll execute whatever you brief in.

Do you have insurance documentation we can share with our procurement teams?

Yes. $5M general liability, $1M professional liability, workers compensation in all 50 states. COI provided within 24 hours of request. Additional insured endorsements available for client procurement teams that require them.
