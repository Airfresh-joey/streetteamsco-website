Street Team Marketing in Los Angeles, Done Right

Los Angeles is one of the most valuable — and most complex — markets in the country for street team marketing. It is enormous, decentralized, and astonishingly diverse, with foot-traffic patterns that vary block by block from the Sunset Strip to the Santa Monica Pier to Downtown's Arts District. Reaching Angelenos at street level takes local knowledge, the right people, and the language fluency to connect authentically — which is exactly what Street Teams Co brings.

We deploy trained, vetted, on-brand street teams and brand ambassadors across the LA metro for sampling, flyering, guerrilla activations, experiential events, and consumer engagement. Our staff are recruited locally in each neighborhood, briefed on your brand, and matched to your audience, with bilingual fluency available in Spanish, Korean, Mandarin, Tagalog, Armenian, and more to match LA's communities.

Whether you are launching a product in Hollywood, sampling in Santa Monica, activating around a venue in Inglewood, or running a multi-neighborhood campaign across the whole metro, we build the team and the plan to make it land.

Where We Activate Across Los Angeles

Our LA street teams know the city's highest-impact zones:

  • Hollywood & the Sunset Strip — Tourism, nightlife, and entertainment foot traffic for high-visibility brand activations.
  • Downtown LA & the Arts District — Crypto.com Arena, L.A. Live, the Convention Center, and a dense, walkable creative core.
  • Santa Monica & Venice — The Pier, Third Street Promenade, and the Boardwalk — some of the most photographed, high-footfall coastline anywhere.
  • Fairfax, Melrose & Koreatown — Trend-forward, youth-skewing, and culturally rich districts ideal for sampling and social-first activations.
  • The San Fernando Valley & Pasadena — Sprawling residential and retail reach beyond the urban core.
  • Venue districts — SoFi Stadium and Intuit Dome in Inglewood, the Rose Bowl in Pasadena, and the Coliseum in Exposition Park.

Frequently Asked Questions: LA Street Team Marketing

What is street team marketing in Los Angeles?

Street team marketing in Los Angeles is face-to-face, boots-on-the-ground brand promotion — trained crews distributing samples and flyers, engaging consumers, driving foot traffic, and creating buzz at high-traffic LA locations. Street Teams Co deploys bilingual street teams, brand ambassadors, and sampling crews across LA, from Hollywood and Downtown to Santa Monica, the Valley, and beyond.

Where do your LA street teams operate?

Across the entire LA metro: Hollywood and the Sunset Strip, Downtown LA and the Arts District, Santa Monica and Venice, the Fairfax and Melrose districts, Koreatown, the San Fernando Valley, Pasadena, Long Beach, Inglewood, and the venue districts around SoFi Stadium, Crypto.com Arena, and the Convention Center. We recruit locally and know the foot-traffic patterns of each neighborhood.

Do you provide bilingual street teams in Los Angeles?

Yes. LA is one of the most diverse markets in the country, and bilingual staffing is often essential. The majority of our available LA street team staff are bilingual in English and Spanish, and we source Korean, Mandarin, Tagalog, Armenian, and other languages to match your target audience and neighborhood.

How much does street team marketing in Los Angeles cost?

Every LA campaign is custom-quoted based on your goals, team size, roles, hours, neighborhoods, and language needs. There are no published rate cards because no two campaigns are alike. Tell us your brief and we will return a detailed staffing plan and a free quote.
