Convention vs Trade Show Staffing: What Is the Difference?

Many brands use "convention staffing" and "trade show staffing" interchangeably, but they serve different needs:

  • Trade show staffing focuses on the exhibitor side: booth brand ambassadors, product demonstrators, lead capture specialists, and promotional models working individual booths on the expo floor
  • Convention staffing covers the entire event: registration teams, wayfinding staff, session monitors, VIP hosts, general event support, AND exhibitor booth staff

If you are an exhibitor staffing a single booth, trade show staffing is what you need. If you are a convention organizer running the entire event, or a large exhibitor with multiple activations beyond your booth, convention staffing is the broader solution. Street Teams Co provides both.

How Our Convention Staffing Process Works

  1. Brief: Share your convention details — event name, dates, venue, staff roles needed, quantities, and any special requirements (product training, dress code, bilingual). We respond within 24 hours with a custom proposal.
  2. Match: We select staff from our local talent pool in your convention city. For exhibitors, staff receive product training before the event. For organizers, staff are briefed on event logistics, floor plans, and attendee protocols.
  3. Deploy: Staff arrive early for on-site briefing and position setup. Our Show-Up Guarantee means every position is filled — if a staff member cannot make it, a qualified replacement is provided at no additional cost.
  4. Report: Real-time GPS check-in verification, timestamped photo documentation, and post-convention performance reports. For exhibitors, lead capture data and engagement metrics are included.
