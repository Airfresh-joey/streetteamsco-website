The Research: Why Ambassadors & Word-of-Mouth Win

Brand ambassadors work because they create the format consumers trust most: a real recommendation from a real person. Selected, sourced findings:

~92% of consumers trust recommendations over advertising
Source: Nielsen, via Ambassador (Nielsen data)
Word of mouth drives 20–50% of all purchasing decisions
Source: McKinsey & Company, via Ambassador
People are ~4x more likely to buy when referred by a friend
Source: Nielsen, via Buyapowa
Word-of-mouth can be several times more effective than paid impressions
Source: BCG, via Ambassador

Figures above are third-party industry research, attributed to their sources. Street Teams Co's "By the Numbers" data is first-party.

Turning Trust Into Trial

The research is consistent: the most trusted, highest-converting marketing format is a real person you trust recommending a product.
