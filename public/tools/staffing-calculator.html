What's Not Included in the Estimate

The calculator estimates direct staff cost only. A complete campaign budget typically includes additional line items:

  • Kit production / branded materials — $500-$5,000 depending on campaign type
  • Permits and venue fees — $0-$10,000 depending on city and venue (festivals expensive)
  • Account management and reporting — typically 15-20% of staff cost
  • Travel / per diem — for multi-city campaigns or non-local staff
  • Technology (GPS tracking, lead capture iPads) — usually included in Street Teams Co engagements
  • Brand training time — 1-2 hours per BA at half rate for product-heavy launches
