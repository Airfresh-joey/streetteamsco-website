Why Phoenix Is a Top Market for Street Teams

  • Massive university audience: Arizona State University is the largest public university in the US by enrollment. ASU's Tempe campus and Mill Avenue corridor offer one of the best campus activation environments in the country for reaching Gen Z consumers
  • Spring training goldmine: 15 MLB teams hold spring training in the Phoenix metro (the Cactus League), concentrating millions of affluent sports fans across the Valley every February and March
  • Premium nightlife market: Old Town Scottsdale is one of the top nightlife destinations in the Southwest, drawing affluent 25 to 45 consumers who spend heavily on entertainment, dining, and lifestyle products
  • Cost advantage: Phoenix activation costs run 25 to 40% below comparable coastal markets while delivering access to a large, diverse, high-quality consumer audience
  • Winter activation season: While northern markets are frozen, Phoenix offers perfect outdoor activation weather from October through April, plus a swelling population of snowbird visitors from cold-weather states
  • Rapid growth: Phoenix is one of the fastest-growing metros in the US, with a population that skews young, active, and brand-receptive

Top Activation Locations in Phoenix

  • Arizona State University (Tempe campus): The single best campus activation location in the American Southwest. Mill Avenue, Hayden Lawn, and the Memorial Union area offer concentrated student foot traffic
  • Old Town Scottsdale: The Valley's premier nightlife, dining, and entertainment district. Ideal for lifestyle, beverage, and entertainment brand campaigns targeting affluent consumers
  • Downtown Phoenix and Roosevelt Row: The urban core's growing arts, dining, and entertainment scene. First Friday art walks draw thousands of engaged consumers monthly
  • Scottsdale Fashion Square: One of the largest malls in the Southwest, surrounded by premium retail and dining. Ideal for luxury and lifestyle brand activations
  • Camelback Corridor: Phoenix's business district along Camelback Road. Excellent for commuter intercept campaigns and B2B street marketing targeting professionals
  • Spring training venues (Scottsdale Stadium, Salt River Fields, Tempe Diablo Stadium): During Cactus League season, these venues and surrounding areas offer concentrated access to affluent, leisure-oriented consumers
  • Kierland Commons and Desert Ridge: North Scottsdale lifestyle retail centers with strong foot traffic from affluent suburban families
Phoenix Insider Tip: Spring training season (mid-February through late March) is the single highest-value activation window in the Phoenix market. Over 3 million fans attend Cactus League games, and the demographics skew affluent, 35 to 65, and leisure-focused. Event perimeter campaigns around spring training venues deliver exceptional ROI for beverage, lifestyle, financial services, and travel brands.
