Product Sampling: Turn the 2028 Games Crowd Into Trial at Scale

Nothing converts like getting your product into a consumer's hand. And no moment in years will deliver more hands to fill than the 2028 Summer Games in Los Angeles. For roughly three weeks, the metro will be packed with millions of warm-weather, high-energy visitors and locals — exactly the relaxed, discovery-minded audience that product sampling is built for. For CPG, beverage, and beauty brands, this is a once-in-a-generation chance to drive trial volume that would take a normal year to match.

What makes LA 2028 special for sampling is the rare overlap of scale, density, and setting. The crowds are enormous, but they are not scattered randomly across the metro — they cluster in predictable, walkable zones like fan zones, watch parties, transit corridors, and beaches. That concentration means a sampling crew can reach thousands of qualified consumers per shift instead of chasing foot traffic. Add the summer heat and the social, celebratory mood, and you have ideal conditions for cold beverages, snacks, and refreshing personal-care products to shine.

How Large-Scale Sampling Works for the 2028 Games

Big sampling numbers come from putting the right crew in the right place with the right logistics. Here is where sampling delivers across Los Angeles during the Games:

  • Venue-adjacent zones — The public areas around venues fill with crowds for hours before and after sessions. Sampling crews catch that captive flow in both directions.
  • Fan zones and viewing plazas — Free public gathering spots offer huge dwell time and the most receptive, discovery-minded crowd of the entire Games.
  • Watch parties — Bars, breweries, and restaurants hosting viewing crowds are ideal for beverage and snack trial with a social audience already in buying mode.
  • Transit hubs and the LAX corridor — Stations and the airport gateway funnel millions through tight choke points — perfect for high-volume intercept sampling.
  • Beaches and boardwalks — Santa Monica, Venice, and the Long Beach waterfront draw all-day crowds craving cold drinks and refreshing products in the summer sun.
  • Cold-chain and full logistics — Refrigerated handling, inventory management, restocking, and consumer data capture so your campaign runs smoothly and reports cleanly.

Bilingual staffing multiplies sampling results. A sample handed over with a warm word in a consumer's own language lands far better, and LA's crowd during the 2028 Games will be intensely international. The majority of our available sampling staff are fully bilingual in English and Spanish, with Korean, Mandarin, Armenian, Tagalog, and additional languages available to connect with every consumer your campaign reaches.

Frequently Asked Questions: Product Sampling

Why is the 2028 Games window ideal for product sampling in Los Angeles?

For roughly three weeks, Los Angeles fills with millions of warm-weather, high-energy consumers concentrated in predictable, walkable zones — fan zones, watch parties, transit corridors, and beaches. That density and the summer setting make it one of the best large-scale sampling environments imaginable, especially for cold beverages, snacks, and refreshing personal-care products that perform in the heat.

What kinds of products do you sample, and can you handle cold-chain?

We run sampling for CPG, beverage, beauty, and personal-care brands, including refrigerated and frozen items. Our crews handle cold-chain logistics, inventory tracking, sample-per-hour targets, and consumer data capture so you get both trial volume and measurable results. We adapt the kit and footprint to your product, whether it is a canned drink, a snack, a skincare sachet, or a full-size giveaway.

Can you run a large-scale, multi-location sampling campaign across LA?

Yes. We scale from a single high-traffic location to a metro-wide program covering venues, fan zones, transit hubs, and beaches simultaneously. One account manager coordinates the whole campaign, with local leads at each site, GPS tracking, timestamped photos, and consolidated reporting so you can see performance everywhere at once.

How is a 2028 sampling campaign priced?

Every sampling campaign is custom-quoted based on the number of locations, team size, sampling volume, hours, logistics needs, and language requirements. There are no published rate cards because no two campaigns are alike. Tell us your brief and we will return a detailed staffing plan and a free quote.
