Watch Party Marketing: The Biggest Secondary Audience of the 2028 Games

Only a fraction of the people following the 2028 Summer Games will ever sit inside a venue. The rest will be watching from a barstool, a brewery patio, a restaurant booth, or a packed sports bar. Across Los Angeles, thousands of these watch parties will run every single day of the Games, and together they form the single largest, most reachable secondary audience of the entire event. For brands and for the venues themselves, that is an enormous, underpriced opportunity.

The magic of watch party marketing is the setting. People at a watch party are relaxed, social, in a celebratory mood, and surrounded by friends — the ideal mindset for trial, conversation, and sharing. They stay for hours, they are easy to find because they cluster in known nightlife and entertainment corridors, and they are far more receptive than someone hustling toward a stadium gate. A well-placed brewery sampling, a branded viewing experience, or a street team funneling walk-ins to a partner bar can convert that mood into real results.

Street Teams Co works both sides of the watch party. For venues, our street teams canvass the surrounding district to drive walk-in traffic and fill seats. For brands, we place brand ambassadors and sampling crews inside partner venues to own the viewing experience with games, giveaways, and sponsor activations. Either way, you turn LA's nightlife into your distribution channel for three unforgettable weeks.

How Watch Party Marketing Works for the 2028 Games

Whether you are a venue trying to pack the house or a brand trying to own the room, here is how we turn watch parties into measurable wins:

  • Drive foot traffic to partner venues — Street teams canvass the surrounding blocks with handouts, incentives, and energy that pulls passersby into your bar, restaurant, or brewery for the next session.
  • In-venue sampling and trial — Sampling crews put your beverage, snack, or product in the hands of a captive, social crowd that is already in buying mode.
  • Sponsor activations and branded viewing experiences — Ambassadors run prize wheels, trivia, photo moments, and branded zones that make your brand the reason people remember the night.
  • Nightlife and entertainment district takeovers — Coordinate a cluster of venues in one corridor so your brand owns an entire district instead of a single room.
  • Brewery and craft-beverage tie-ins — LA's brewery scene is built for watch parties. Patio sampling, tap takeovers, and co-branded experiences thrive in this format.
  • Content and social amplification — Our teams capture the energy and generate user-generated content, extending a single night into a far larger digital footprint.

Bilingual staffing strengthens every watch party. LA's nightlife crowd is deeply diverse, and the international visitors arriving for the 2028 Games will be celebrating their own teams in their own languages. The majority of our available watch party staff are fully bilingual in English and Spanish, with Korean, Mandarin, Armenian, Tagalog, and additional languages available to connect with every group in the room.

Frequently Asked Questions: Watch Party Marketing

What does watch party marketing involve during the 2028 Games?

Watch party marketing means activating the bars, restaurants, breweries, and entertainment venues where Angelenos and visitors gather to watch the 2028 Summer Games. We deploy street teams to drive foot traffic to partner venues, sampling crews to put product in hands, and brand ambassadors to run in-venue sponsor activations like games, giveaways, and branded viewing experiences.

Can you help a venue or a brand fill seats during the 2028 Games?

Yes. For venues, our street teams canvass the surrounding nightlife and entertainment districts to drive walk-in traffic to your watch party. For brands, we place ambassadors and sampling teams inside partner venues to own the viewing experience. Both approaches turn the millions of casual watchers into a captive, engaged audience for your campaign.

Which LA nightlife and entertainment districts do you cover?

We cover watch-party-heavy districts across the metro including Downtown LA and the Arts District, Hollywood, West Hollywood, Santa Monica and the Westside, Long Beach, Pasadena, the South Bay, and the bar and brewery corridors near every major venue. We can run a single flagship venue or a coordinated multi-district program under one account manager.

How is watch party marketing priced for the 2028 Games?

Every watch party program is custom-quoted based on the number of venues, team size, hours, roles, and language needs. There are no published rate cards because no two campaigns are alike. Tell us your brief and we will return a detailed staffing plan and a free quote.
