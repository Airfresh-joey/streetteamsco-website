Event Staffing at SoFi Stadium & Hollywood Park

SoFi Stadium is the gravitational center of Los Angeles's modern sports and entertainment scene — home to the Rams and Chargers, a marquee concert venue, and a confirmed host site for both the 2026 World Cup and the 2028 Summer Games. Anchored alongside the Intuit Dome and the Kia Forum in the Hollywood Park development, it draws some of the densest, highest-energy crowds anywhere in the LA metro, with foot traffic building for hours before events and lingering long after.

That concentration is exactly what makes the SoFi district one of the most valuable activation footprints in Southern California. Crowds funnel through a relatively compact set of pedestrian corridors, plazas, parking structures, and rideshare zones — captive audiences moving on predictable paths, the ideal environment for street teams, sampling crews, and high-visibility brand ambassadors.

Street Teams Co maintains an established, active staff network throughout Inglewood and the surrounding South LA communities. Our local teams know the area's foot-traffic patterns, the best intercept points around Hollywood Park, and how crowds flow between the stadium, the arena, and the nearby entertainment venues.

Activation Roles We Staff at SoFi Stadium

  • Brand ambassadors — Trained, bilingual reps for product education, lead capture, and consumer engagement across the stadium perimeter and Hollywood Park.
  • Street teams — High-energy crews working the parking structures, rideshare zones, and pedestrian corridors on event days.
  • Product sampling — Fan-targeted sampling of beverages, snacks, and promotional items, with cold-chain logistics for summer events.
  • Event & hospitality staff — Registration, hospitality, crowd flow, and sponsor lounge support across the venue cluster.
  • Guerrilla & experiential — Attention-grabbing street-level activations and installations around the Hollywood Park development.
  • Bilingual staff — English/Spanish plus Korean, Tagalog, Mandarin, and more for the international crowds SoFi draws.

Bilingual staffing is essential here: Inglewood and the surrounding communities are deeply diverse, and the global crowds arriving for the World Cup and 2028 Games are even more international. The majority of our available SoFi-area team is fully bilingual in English and Spanish, with additional languages available to match your audience.

Frequently Asked Questions: SoFi Stadium Staffing

Can you provide brand ambassadors and event staff at SoFi Stadium?

Yes. Street Teams Co staffs brand activations, sampling, street teams, and promotional teams at and around SoFi Stadium and the surrounding Hollywood Park development. We deploy on the stadium perimeter, in the parking and rideshare zones, and across the pedestrian corridors that fill before and after every event — for concerts, NFL games, and major events through the 2026 World Cup and 2028 Summer Games.
