Frequently Asked Questions

How much does SXSW event staffing cost?

SXSW staffing is custom-quoted based on roles, team size, and how many festival days you cover. A weekend street team prices very differently from a two-week brand-house program. Tell us your plan and we will send a free itemized quote.

Do I need an official SXSW sponsorship to activate?

No. Many of the most talked-about SXSW campaigns are unofficial: street teams, sampling, pop-ups, and venue takeovers in public and private spaces around downtown. We design and staff programs that work within Austin rules without official sponsorship.

When should I book SXSW 2027 staff?

By January for first pick. Austin talent gets claimed early for March, and multi-week programs need training and logistics lead time. Rush staffing during the festival is possible but selection is limited.

Do you handle City of Austin permits for street activity?

Yes. We manage permitting and compliance for sampling, distribution, and street-team activity, and we brief teams on where activation is and is not allowed during the festival.

Can you staff both interactive week and music week?

Yes. We build rosters that flex across the full run — heavier tech-audience coverage early, music-crowd coverage later — with shift rotations that keep teams fresh.
