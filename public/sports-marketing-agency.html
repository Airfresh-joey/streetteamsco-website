Sports Marketing Services That Reach Fans Where the Energy Is Highest

For brands that know how to activate in this environment, sports marketing delivers some of the highest ROI in experiential marketing. Street Teams Co specializes in connecting brands with fans at the moment they are most receptive to engagement.

The Tailgate Marketing Playbook

Tailgate marketing is one of the highest-engagement activation formats in sports marketing. Here is how we maximize ROI for brands in the tailgate environment:

Timing and Positioning

Our tailgate teams arrive 3-4 hours before kickoff and deploy across the most active parking lots and tailgate zones. We map out the venue's parking layout in advance, identify the highest-traffic entry corridors and most popular tailgate zones, and position our teams accordingly. For NFL games, this means covering the main lot entrances, premium tailgate areas, and the walkways fans use to approach the stadium.

Branded Vehicle Setup

A branded vehicle serves as a mobile activation hub in the tailgate environment. It gives your brand a visible presence, provides storage for sampling inventory, creates a destination for fan engagement, and establishes a professional-looking activation footprint in an otherwise chaotic parking lot. We coordinate branded vehicle wraps, tent setups, table displays, and signage that cut through the visual noise of a tailgate.

Fan Engagement That Feels Authentic

Tailgating is about community, celebration, and shared passion for the team. Our tailgate marketing teams understand that heavy-handed sales pitches fall flat in this environment. Instead, they lead with genuine fan energy, shared game excitement, and product experiences that feel like a natural part of the tailgate. When your sampling staff high-five fans, wear team colors, and genuinely celebrate first downs, the brand-consumer connection becomes authentic.
