Why Booth Staffing Decides ROI

With most attendees holding buying authority and the majority more likely to buy from exhibitors they meet in person, the people at your booth are the conversion engine. Trained event staff and brand ambassadors who qualify leads, capture data, and follow a process turn foot traffic into pipeline — while unstaffed or untrained booths leak the 80% of leads that never get followed up.
