What Is a Field Marketing Agency?

A field marketing agency is a specialized firm that takes your brand out of the office and into the real world. Field marketing puts trained representatives in the places where your target consumers live, work, shop, and play, creating direct face-to-face interactions that digital marketing simply cannot replicate. From product demonstrations in grocery stores to lead generation at industry conferences, field marketing is the execution arm that turns marketing strategies into real-world consumer engagement.

Field marketing has experienced a significant resurgence as brands discover that digital-only strategies leave gaps in consumer trust and product understanding. A consumer who has held your product, tasted your food, or spoken with a knowledgeable representative is significantly more likely to become a customer than someone who has only seen an ad. Research shows that face-to-face marketing interactions are 34 times more effective at driving purchase intent than digital impressions alone.

Street Teams Co operates as a full-service field marketing agency, providing the trained personnel, logistics management, and performance reporting infrastructure that brands need to execute field campaigns at any scale. Whether you need a single demo specialist for a weekend retail event or a 200-person team for a national product launch, we have the network, systems, and experience to deliver.

How Our Field Marketing Process Works

Executing field marketing campaigns requires meticulous planning, reliable staffing, and disciplined on-the-ground management. Here is how Street Teams Co delivers field marketing campaigns from briefing to reporting:

  1. Campaign Planning: We analyze your objectives, target audience, product details, and geographic requirements to develop a field marketing plan. This includes staffing recommendations, location selection, shift scheduling, and KPI definitions. We identify the right field rep profiles—demo specialists, sampling ambassadors, canvassers, or lead gen reps—for your specific campaign type.
  2. Staff Selection & Training: From our 10,000+ vetted network, we select field reps who match your brand requirements including demographics, language skills, industry experience, and geographic proximity. Each rep completes a custom training program covering your product, talking points, engagement techniques, compliance requirements, and reporting procedures.
  3. Deployment & Field Management: Our field managers oversee every shift, handling check-ins, materials distribution, on-site coaching, and real-time problem-solving. GPS tracking verifies that your team is where they should be, when they should be there. You receive live updates throughout each activation via our reporting platform.
  4. Reporting & Optimization: After every shift, you receive detailed reports with engagement metrics, samples distributed, leads captured, conversion data, photos, and consumer feedback. For ongoing programs, we continuously optimize location selection, staffing levels, engagement scripts, and scheduling based on performance data.

Frequently Asked Questions About Field Marketing

What does a field marketing agency do?

A field marketing agency deploys trained marketing staff into the field—retail stores, events, public spaces, and neighborhoods—to engage consumers directly with your brand. Services include product demonstrations, sampling campaigns, in-store merchandising, lead generation, door-to-door marketing, and direct-to-consumer activations. The agency handles recruiting, training, scheduling, logistics, and performance reporting.

How much does field marketing cost?

Field marketing costs depend on the market, skill level, and campaign type. Most campaigns have a 4-hour shift minimum, and multi-day and ongoing programs receive volume discounts. We provide custom proposals based on your campaign.

What is the difference between field marketing and digital marketing?

Field marketing takes place in the physical world—at retail locations, events, and public spaces—where staff interact face-to-face with consumers. Digital marketing operates online through ads, social media, email, and content. Field marketing excels at product trial, sensory experiences, and building personal connections. The most effective strategies combine both channels.

How do you recruit and train field marketing staff?

We maintain a vetted network of 10,000+ field marketing professionals across 1,000+ US cities. Every team member passes background checks, communication assessments, and live interviews. For each campaign, we create custom training covering product details, brand messaging, engagement scripts, and reporting procedures.

Can field marketing work for B2B companies?

Yes. Field marketing is highly effective for B2B companies, particularly at trade shows, industry conferences, office park activations, and business district canvassing. Our B2B field teams specialize in lead qualification, product demos for decision-makers, and appointment setting.

How do you measure field marketing performance?

Performance is measured through samples distributed, consumer interactions logged, leads captured, demo-to-sale conversion rates, retail sell-through data, GPS-verified location coverage, shift compliance tracking, and qualitative consumer feedback. We provide real-time dashboards and post-campaign reports.

What areas do your field marketing teams cover?

Street Teams Co has active field marketing teams in 1,000+ US cities across all 50 states. We cover every major metro area and most mid-size markets. For campaigns in smaller markets, we recruit and train local talent 2-3 weeks before activation.

How quickly can you deploy field marketing teams?

Standard deployment takes 2-3 weeks for staff selection, training, and logistics coordination. Rush deployment in major metros is available with 5-7 business days notice. Ongoing programs with established teams can scale up with 48-72 hours notice for additional shifts or markets.
