The Research: Why Guerrilla Sticks

Guerrilla marketing's edge is memorability and cost-efficiency — it creates an experience worth sharing. Selected, sourced findings:

~90% find guerrilla campaigns memorable, vs ~30% for traditional ads
Source: guerrilla marketing research, via The Growth Scene
~4.5–5x average ROI on guerrilla campaigns
Source: guerrilla marketing research, via Persuasion Nation
~63% of consumers who experience guerrilla marketing share it
Source: guerrilla marketing research, via The Growth Scene
~70% say guerrilla tactics feel more personalized than traditional ads
Source: guerrilla marketing research, via Persuasion Nation

Figures above are third-party industry research, attributed to their sources. Street Teams Co's "By the Numbers" data is first-party.
